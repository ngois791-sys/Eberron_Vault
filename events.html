History

Timeline

The history of the world, from creation to the present day. Dates are counted relative to Year 0, the start of our campaign. Oldest at the top.

  • Prehistory — Times Unknown

    • The world is created by the Progenitor Dragons: Khyber, Siberys, and Eberron.
    • The first lands created are for dragons, the oldest of all beings.
    • Draksholm is created for the dragonborn.
    • Khorvaire is created for humanoids.

  • ~7,000 years before

    • While civilization on Khorvaire is in its infancy, it is thriving on Draksholm.

  • ~5,000 years before

    • The seers of Draksholm put forth all their powers of divination and record their visions as The Draconic Prophecy.
    • Festarik's draconian experiments with Khyber's magic nearly destroy Draksholm. Only a few dragonborn make it out, fleeing to Khorvaire under the leadership of Cuprir, Festarik's mate, while Festarik is trapped in stasis for thousands of years.
    • Cuprir brings a fragment of The Draconic Prophecy with her, and it ends up on the Stele of the Ancestors that the kobold monks of Q'barra protect.
    • Dragonborn first land on the eastern shores of what will become Q'barra. Over millennia, these dragonborn become kobolds and lizardfolk.

  • 1,700–1,800 years before

    • Solamnia is the dominant kingdom in Khorvaire.
    • Khardam Silvereye leads the battle against the resurrected dragon Kymrys and his army of the undead. He dies defeating the dragon.
    • Solamnia eventually collapses.

  • 900 years before

    • Traelor and Oalian team up to oppose the monsters of The Demon Wastes.
    • Traelor creates The Labyrinth to seal The Demon Wastes. He makes a pact with Khyber to accomplish the feat.
    • Traelor turns on Oalian, and their fight ends with Oalian being defeated and turned into a large tree.

  • 700 years before

    • The kingdom of Galifar is founded.

  • 600–650 years before

    • The Church of the Silver Flame launches crusades against The Dark Pack and House Vol. Tira defeats Valdren, the leader of the werewolves of The Dark Pack.
    • The Dark Pack goes into hiding, operating from the shadows.
    • Tira Miron sacrifices herself to defeat The Great Demon, and Flamekeep is founded on the spot where she was immolated.

  • 350 years before

    • Inarieth defeats the Elder Tempest but is killed in the attempt.

  • 100–110 years before

    • King Jarot il'Wynarn dies.
    • Monarchs start raising questions about the line of succession for the kingdom of Galifar.
    • The Last War starts as Galifar fractures into Breland, Karrnath, Aundair, Thrane, and Cyre as individual kingdoms.

  • 60 years before

    • Leroy is born.

  • 32 years before

    • Ziolda is born.

  • 29 years before

    • Leroy's son, Arel, is born.

  • 27 years before

    • Leroy's daughter, Aurora, is born.

  • 22 years before

    • House Cannith discovers obsidian dragonshards and starts using them for warforged experiments.
    • Flynt is born.

  • 20 years before

    • Leroy's wife and daughter are killed by werewolves, while his son is carried away by them.

  • 19 years before

    • Kitt is born.

  • 15 years before

    • Leroy finds the Crimson Knights of Khorvaire and begins his training.

  • 12 years before

    • Flynt runs away from home to Cyre and changes his name.

  • 5 years before

    • Flynt leaves Cyre for the Eldeen Reaches to learn druidcraft.

  • 4 years before

    • The Mourning occurs, ending The Last War and destroying Cyre. Cyre is widely called The Mournlands.
    • Ziolda is killed and reanimated. She starts making her way to Flamekeep, though she is unaware of her actions.
    • Kitt finds Peanut and the bones of a dragon that she makes into a staff. Her latent dragonmark is awakened.
    • Leroy and Ronan infiltrate Cyre during the war, tracking the werewolves that killed Leroy's family. Leroy is caught in the mists of the Mourning, magically changing him.
  • The present day

    Year 0 — Campaign Start

    • Ziolda regains full control and awareness of her body and sets off on a mission for the Church of the Sacred Flame.
    • Kitt sets out from her tribe, ready to journey to Arcanix.
    • Leroy undergoes the hemocraft rite to become a full member of the Crimson Knights of Khorvaire and heads east to investigate Lake Dark.
