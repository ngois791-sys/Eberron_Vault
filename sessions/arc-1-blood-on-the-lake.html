The Chronicle · Arc 1

Arc 1 — Blood on the Lake

Session 1 Session 2 Session 3 Interlude 1 Session 4

Session 1

Last time, we flashed back four years to the end of the Last War, on the day of the Mourning when an explosion in Cyre covered the entire country in dense mists hundreds of feet high and the country became a wasteland.

Leroy was in Cyre hunting for werewolves to avenge the deaths of his wife and two kids when the mist overtook him, turning his skin grey and teleporting him away from his friend and hunting partner, Ronin. Leroy landed on a sword with Elvish-looking runes around the hilt, which he unwisely removed from his leg before limping further into the mists.

Kitt and her mentor, Weyla, discovered the ancient lair of some great beast after a chunk of rare and powerful Siberys dragonshard fell to the earth, landing in this lair. Kitt used a bone from the ancient beast to shift a rock trapping a tiny fastieth, which became her constant companion, Peanut. When the Mourning happened, Weyla sacrificed the dragonshard to create a powerful Wind Wall to protect herself, Kitt, and Peanut until it receded to the Cyran border.

Ziolda, having retrieved schematics of the Cyran warforged colossus war machines, prepared to try to get inside one to destroy it from within. But the charge, led by her captain and mentor, Kel, ended in disaster as the massive machine decimated the Thrane forces before the Mourning sent tendrils of mist into it. The colossus collapsed and exploded, sending a shock wave over the remainder of the Thranes, including Ziolda.

Ziolda awoke four years later in Flamekeep, the capital of Thrane and the center of the Silver Flame faith, having no memory of any work she had apparently been doing in the meantime as a paladin chosen by the Silver Flame. She discussed this with a page named Siu, who said it would be best to keep her amnesia quiet until she found some more answers. Ziolda was sent to the Nightwood, in Karrnath, where there were reports of wandering zombies raised by followers of the Blood of Vol, and a huge black bird that was attacking and robbing Silver Flame clerics.

Leroy was forced to relive the day his family was killed as he went through the Hunter's Bane ritual to become a member of the Crimson Knights of Khorvaire. He then received a contract from one of the order's leaders, Bervodd Longtooth, to go investigate what seems to be drawing zombies to the village of Lakeside in Karrnath.

Kitt set out to look for the magic college in the west, traveling with Peanut and the Circle of the Primeval gear Weyla had given her from her own adventuring days. After traveling to Gatherhold and learning that the college is called Arcanix and that it exists in western Aundair, she proclaimed herself, "good at this adventuring thing" and went north to Karrnath to avoid the mists of the Mournlands.

Everybody converged in the Nightwood when zombies attacked. After defeating the zombies, you made camp and talked about your individual goals and how they might work together. Early in the morning you heard fighting in the distance. When you went to see what was going on, you found two knights on the ground, robbed of their horses and weapons. The knights informed you that they were Aelbard and Gaius, knights of the Order of the Emerald Claw.

You then went looking for the bandits with disastrous results. After Ziolda and Leroy were nearly killed, Kitt surrendered and the bandits took all of your money. Wanting revenge and your money back, you healed up the knights and ambushed the bandits in their camp, defeating them.

Session 2

Last time, you searched the small horde of the bandits in the Nightwood. You retrieved the stolen swords of Aelbard and Gaius, knights of the Order of the Emerald Claw, and the two knights took their leave, telling you that you would be welcome in their order's headquarters in Rekkenmark.

You continued searching and found a scroll written in some language you couldn't comprehend, as well as two Blood of Vol signet rings bearing some sort of magical aura. Eventually, you put the rings on and received the encoded message in them, telling you how to get to the Blood of Vol's coven in these woods.

You approached some old ruins on a hilltop and alerted the two Blood of Vol members there to your presence and to the fact that you had found the rings and knew the message they contained. One of the cultists disappeared to warn the rest of the coven while you regrouped.

Killing the remaining watchman and finding your way through the illusory entrance, you entered the hideout and began clearing it out, picking up a few Eberron dragonshards along the way. When you got to the leader's room, you found him burning the research and communications on his desks. While Leroy and Ziolda fought him, Kitt put out the fires and salvaged as many of the documents as possible.

The research indicated that the cultists were experimenting the zombified forms of different species, trying to use traits from one to make another stronger. There was also the beginning of a message to someone named Lord Soth saying that "Unfortunately, My Lord, he was resurrected. I don't know how, but they say it was the kid."

You defeated the ogre zombie the cultists were working on and picked up some spare Blood of Vol robes before heading out to Lakeside to deal with the contract Leroy had been given: to investigate what was drawing an increasing number of zombies to the small fishing village.

In Lakeside, you met Sylvari, an older half-elf druid who offered half of her mother's Valenar twinblade as payment for helping the town. She also taught Kitt about herbalism, brewing healing potions, and mentioned that many druids gather in the Eldeen Reaches, before Kitt and Peanut went and got high in her garden. Leroy went to silver his new twinblade at the local blacksmith and Ziolda went to the tavern to ask about two strangers that had recently been to town.

The strangers had camped on the northeastern edge of town and were dressed in robes matching the ones you took from the cultists. They apparently claimed they were in town to help defend the villagers. But after they left, the zombies started coming. Sylvari is going to help get as many people out of the town as possible while you investigate and deal with the approaching horde.

Session 3

Last time, you all prepared to defend the fishing village of Lakeside against the approaching zombie threat while trying to figure out what was causing it. After Sylvari helped evacuate as many villagers as possible, you went to the cemetery. Instead of fighting you, the approaching zombies seemed more interested in digging up bodies and dragging them away.

You found the hilt of a sword buried in one of the graves, wrapped in leather and rosebush that concealed the runes carved into the hilt beneath. Kitt broke the hilt in the hopes that it would stop the zombies, but it didn’t.

After Leroy slowed down one of the zombies and the rest were destroyed, he decided to let the last zombie go to see where it would go. It led you to the town square, where more zombies were dragging freshly killed bodies from the direction of the tavern and heading toward Lake Dark. Leroy pretended to be a zombie before forcing one of them into a well. You found another wrapped hilt in a tree and took it with you to the lake. The zombies seemed to drawn to this one.

At the lake, you saw roughly forty zombies gathered on the docks, along with some fresh victims. Leroy lured some of them into a boat, set it and the dock on fire, then pushed the boat out into the lake. While Ziolda held off any new zombies approaching, Kitt and Leroy lured the rest into one of the buildings before trapping them and setting the building on fire along with the hilt. When Leroy went to help Ziolda, Kitt found another wrapped hilt under the docks, along with two disembodied hands that attacked her when she tried to take it.

With the zombie threat taken care of, you went to the tavern, where zombies had killed and dragged away any citizens taking refuge there. Inside the flue of the fireplace, you found a fourth hilt hidden. After making sure the fire wouldn't spread, you went back to Irontown, where the villagers were taking refuge. You informed Sylvari of the situation in Lakeside before heading out on the road again, back toward Flamekeep and Cardell Keep, with the plan of splitting up in Thaliost and meeting back at a Gold Dragon Inn in Daskaran.

Interlude 1

Session 4

Last time, you made your way from Irontown and Lakeside back through the Nightwood and on to Rekkenmark. There, you came face-to-face with three Blood of Vol cultists in the street. But knowing they had the people of Rekkenmark on their side, you chose to move on without incident.

You then crossed over the New White Arch Bridge, still under construction, which spans Scions Sound from Rekkenmark to Thaliost. On the Thaliost side, you heard the sound of a distant protest turning violent, but you were assured by the local guards that they could handle it without you.

You then split up, with Leroy going back to the Crimson Knights and Kitt and Ziolda going to Flamekeep.

Leroy showed Bervodd Longtooth the runes from the enchanted hilts, and Bervodd informed him that they were instructions for the zombies written in Abyssal. Bervodd said that he and Istar would look into why the Blood of Vol would have an interest in Lakeside. Leroy received a dirty look from Vitara Shei because of his twinblade, and Bervodd brought him inside to discuss werewolves.

After giving Leroy a history lesson about the Order of the Lycan, he offered Leroy a chance to join and become a werebear so he could better hunt down werewolves. Leroy accepted and went through a painful ritual called the Taming. Bervodd charged Leroy with getting more information about werewolves from the Church of the Silver Flame, and Leroy said he might have an in there.

In Flamekeep, Ziolda spoke with Jaela Daran about the Blood of Vol and showed her one of the hilts. High Priest Gloz took the hilt to the Council of Cardinals in the hope that it would help him convince them to declare war on the Blood of Vol. Ziolda then spoke with Jaela about how she came to Flamekeep and her missing memories. It turned out Ziolda has just showed up at the cathedral with the Silver Flame pendant around her neck and a severe head wound. She was then put to work leading a few knights to take care of local bandits. Apparently, they reported her having power beyond what she knows she can do, including making all of their weapons shine with holy light.

She then met with Queen Diani il’Wynarn, who asked her to help keep Thrane and the rest of Khorvaire stable and to help her convince Jaela and the Council of Cardinals not to declare war and to put efforts into rebuilding instead. Ziola reaffirmed the oaths to her country she had taken as a soldier, and Diani made her a knight. Her first job for the queen is to go find a way to bring peace to Thaliost as a group known as the Dragonhawks has started violently opposing Thrane rule there.

Meanwhile, Kitt's barbaric appearance, along with Peanut, nearly got her arrested as she went looking for the library. But she was saved by another halfling who called himself Flynt. Flynt polymorphed Peanut into a mouse and helped Kitt find the library. Peanut, as a mouse, rode on Kitt, who wild shaped into a cat and rode on Flynt's shoulder while Flynt's falcon, Ferno, flew overhead. Flynt helped Kitt decipher the Abyssal writing from the hilts and the Infernal writing on the scroll taken from the bandit camp, which turned out to be instructions for casting the Hellish Rebuke spell.

While they were talking and in the library, Flynt noticed Kitt's desire to learn more magic and before they parted, he offered her an old, leaf-bound book he got from druids in the Eldeen Reaches from which he had learned most of his druidcraft. He also gave her the rune combination for his speaking stone in case she ever got a hold of one and wanted to get in touch.

Ziolda and Kitt met back up and left Flamekeep for Daskaran to meet up with Leroy. Along the way, they stopped for the night near the Face of Tira and had a shared dream of Kitt drowning, being pulled into dark water by the back of her neck. Ziolda dove in to save her, pulling her back to the surface.

In Daskaran, Leroy revealed that he now has "a time of the month" and said "Come to the back alley and I'll show you." Thankfully, it was just his werebear form that he wanted to show, and you all caught up, making plans to get some information on werewolves from Flamekeep before heading back to Thaliost.
