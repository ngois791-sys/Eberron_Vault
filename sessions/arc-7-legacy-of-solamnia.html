Session 34

Last time, you headed into The Gloaming while Oswald stole the Greenblade. Oswald appeared with blood on his mouth and some scales missing, but he had the sword. You discovered that the writing on the blade is Solamnic, making the sword ancient. You each tried connecting with the sword, but it only had any response to Kitt, though it was weak.

As you went through The Gloaming, the sky turned to perpetual twilight and the world went silent except for small noises in the surrounding trees. When you made camp the first night, Kitt got a 28 and Flynt crit on Perception while on watch. They saw three glowing lights darting around out in the forest. When Leroy took over watch, he got a double crit on Perception and saw shadows slithering along the ground.

Everyone woke up to fight shadowy tendrils that tried to take people away into the forest while three of the tendrils puppeted the corpses of gnomish rangers with glowing lights in their mouths like lampreys. Petrus was nearly taken by the tendrils, but Ziolda managed to cut him free and bring him back.

After you drove off the tendrils, everyone made for the edge of The Gloaming as quickly as possible. After you crossed the boundary, the tendrils stopped pursuing you.

You eventually reached your destination, the ruins of an ancient Solamnic fortress that has been almost entirely buried by centuries of mud and rockslides from the mountains. Only the top of a tower can be seen, but leading to a hatch in the tower, you saw the footprints of clawed feet along with a single set of humanoid boot prints disappearing into the hatch.

Session 35

Last time, you explored the ruins of an ancient Solamnic fort, looking for the source of the draconian footprints and the lone pair of humanoid boot prints that led into it.

You snuck down into the buried fortress and found the entrance hall was blocked by a mudslide that smashed in the doors and filled the middle of the room. Kitt managed to squeeze through a gap near the top after getting caught once by the blade strapped to her back that she's still getting used to.

After she was through, you decided to make the hole bigger so Ziolda and Leroy could fit through as well. But when you started to widen the hole, the ceiling collapsed. Ziolda, Peanut, and Flynt made it through to Kitt, but Leroy and Izzy did not, and the party was split.

Kitt, Ziolda, Peanut, and Flynt pushed on while Leroy and Izzy were forced to hide from the draconians closing in on their position. The crew pushing ahead found that there were more draconians further in and decided to take another path. Peanut and Kitt got trapped in the tendrils of a plant filling the old mess hall and kitchen. When you had Flynt use fire to get Kitt out, the main body of the plant inside the kitchen started attacking.

Meanwhile, Leroy and Izzy snuck back through the fort and looped around the other way. They found list that looked like a ledger, a map of Solamnia, and a soldier's account before meeting back up with the others, who just finished fighting their way out of the plant.

Ziolda translated the Solamnic and you learned that the battle in which Khardam and Kymrys the undead dragon killed each other took place on The Whispering Plains, located on the southern shore of Lake Palanthas. The leger said that Solamnia was gathering their forces in Rosewind. Cross-referencing these places with the modern map showed you that Rosewind is now essentially the town of Delethorn, while the Whispering Plains are under the waters of what is now called Lake Galifar.

You all pressed on to the last room, a great hall with an adjoining room where the draconians were. Leroy and Ziolda burst through the door to pin one of them to the wall behind it. There were five draconians and a human, but the human scooped up a pile of papers and disappeared through a portal with one of the lead draconians.

After you defeated all of the draconians, you searched for any scroll the human might have missed, and you found that they want to bring back the dragon, and they were given information on how to do it from someone called The Nomad.

Session 36

Last time, you left the Silver Moon rangers to further investigate the dark tendrils that attacked you in the Gloamwood and you headed north with Izzy and Flynt to Greenheart.

You discussed the visions you had in the Great Diamond and briefly considered stabbing Oalian, the archdruid who was turned into a tree, with the Greenblade that Oswald stole, to see if the druidic magic would have an effect on the blade. You decided against that and Kitt instead tried to investigate the ley lines around Oalian to see if the sword could absorb some of that power. The conclusion she came away with was that it might be able to, but only if she learns how to control ley magic from Oalian's lost spellbook.

You then headed to the town of Delethorn on the shore of Lake Galifar and asked around about any sign of cultists or draconians. No one in town had seen anything, but you followed a rat to the ruins of an old cellar where an account of the battle directed you to the general area where it took place. While in Delethorn, you also visited a curios shop and bought a lot of goodberry taffies and saw "Jake the Alligator Man", a half man-half alligator creature on display.

You headed out onto the lake to find the location of the battlefield. Izzy, Peanut, and Zelda stayed on the boat while the rest of you swam down to Kymrys's skeleton. As you approached, you saw a woman being attacked by a dragon eel that had been living in the rib cage of the dragon. When you got the dragon eel off her arm, you saw that her arm was a lobster claw.

After killing the dragon eel, you investigated the area around the dragon skeleton and found that many of its bones were carved with Solamnic writing promising revenge for the enemy taking Khardam's body during their retreat after the dragon's fall. There was also a monument built in honor of Khardam in the absence of a body to bury. From the angle of the remains of war machines on the lakebed, you deduced that the enemy came from the direction of the Blackcaps, the mountains that divide Aundair from Breland.

You then followed the lobster-armed woman to her underwater village and realized that Jake the Alligator Man and the "mermaids" that people have seen in Delethorn were actually these people, who call themselves The Deeplings. Their ancestors were the result of experiments done at Arcanix to give people aquatic adaptations. The result of these "failed experiments" was that they all have an inherited magical mutation, but that mutation manifests differently in each person as various aquatic features. Their leader is one of the "successful experiments" who can live above the surface for a short time. Her ancestors are the ones people believed to be merpeople.

You talked with the Deeplings about guarding the dragon's bones against cultists who might want to reanimate them and said you would bring their existence to the attention of the wizards at Arcanix. You also took a bone from the dragon's skeleton in case it can't be reanimated without all of its bones.

You went to Arcanix and learned that the founder of the school, Eladan Ardosar, had discovered the research that created the Deeplings and put a stop to it. You also learned that he was the one from Kitt's vision in the Great Diamond, who discovered the Snarl and built the college around the magical anomaly.

While you talked with the faculty, Izzy went to the artificers with some dragon eel skin and returned with armor for Peanut as well as bracers for Leroy and Kitt that give lightning resistance.

With assurances that the wizards of Arcanix will be on the lookout for cultist activity around the dragon, you headed south to the Blackcaps to search for the cultists.

Session 37

Last time, you headed into the Blackcaps to search for the cult responsible for creating draconians and seeking to resurrect the dragon Kymrys. You started hiking through the mountains and Flynt got off to an auspicious start, getting cut up on the first obstacle you faced, a slightly sloped wall of shale. Everyone else made it up just fine.

Then you found yourselves at the edge of a field of the mysterious black rock the mountains are named after. The rock seems to give off a heat of its own, and it burned Leroy when he started to walk across it. You chipped away some of the rock and found that it retained its heat, so you wrapped it up and put it in your packs. Then Ziolda and Kitt, as a giant eagle, flew the party safely across the black rock.

You made it across a large ravine and made camp for the first night. In the morning, you started seeing signs of activity. There were claw marks in the stone that looked about the size of a young dragon or a similar creature. Then you came across two cultists who had been torn to shreds. Each of them had a black metal pendant stylized like a dragon head and bearing an inscription on the back. The inscriptions were damaged, but you could make out, "Hail Za…….lack…he…agon Bel…"

You continued down this path until you heard a thunderstorm approaching. You had to cross a narrow ridge in the wind and rain to get to shelter. Everyone but Ziolda was tied together, and when Flynt and Izzy slipped, they nearly dragged Leroy and Peanut with them, but Leroy and Peanut managed to stop their fall and pull them back up.

When you reached a collapsed area, Ferno teleported Flynt and Peanut across, then Kitt, as a gorilla, threw Izzy like a soccer ball. Izzy slipped on the wet stone on the other side and nearly fell, but Flynt managed to grab her and pull her to safety. His one moment of good luck on the whole journey.

When everyone was safely across, you passed under streams of falling water that Kitt diverted with Mold Earth, then you made one last push up a muddy slope to a cave. But out in the open atop a mountain, you were at risk of being struck by lightning. Leroy, Kitt, and Peanut were somewhat protected by their new dragon eel armor, and Ziolda, despite having a literal lightning rod on her back, managed to be struck very little. Izzy and Flynt were struck several times, with Flynt being struck every single round until he reached the cave.

While Izzy took care of Flynt, the rest of you examined the cave and found that it was rich in moonstone, which is said to cause lucid dreams. That night, you each had strange dreams. Ziolda dreamed about Alderdia showing her that Khardam and Kymrys's spirits were fighting. Ziolda fought her way toward Khardam, trying to forge some sort of connection. When Khardam looked up at her, Kymrys struck, and Khardam let out a roar of pain that sounded like a dragon.

Kitt dreamed about the golden draconian you encountered in the Solamnic ruins. It entered a cavern where cultists and draconians were gathered for a ritual to corrupt a blue dragon egg and create more draconians. Kitt saw that the more grotesque draconians all had a Khyber dragonshard hanging around their necks while others, like the gold one, wore strange black stones. These draconians seemed less enthusiastic about the ritual and seemed to show respect to the gold one. Upon close inspection, the stones on their necks had a flickering orange light deep inside, like a flame. Kitt also saw that the cultists had a collection of eggs that seemed different than dragon eggs.

Leroy dreamed about his son wandering through the mists of the Mournlands. After a brief reunion, Arel told Leroy to run as the mists parted to reveal the moon and Arel started to turn into a werewolf. When Leroy woke up, he asked Kitt and Ziolda, but especially Ziolda as a soldier, to do what needs to be done if Arel is too far gone, because he won't have the strength to do it himself.

Flynt said that he had a dream that he was flying over the Blackcaps. He felt very hot and assumed that he was seeing through Ferno's eyes, but then Ferno flew in front of him.

You pressed on with your journey and found a half of a cultist surrounded by scorch marks that Izzy said looked like they were caused by spells. Leroy found a note on the body that said, "Enwin Fizzlethread. Black Pit Market. Two greens from the Deepwood."

You were then attacked by five wyverns. Leroy led two of them off to give the rest of the party an easier time getting to cover. Kitt severely confused one of them with magic. Izzy and Flynt set one on fire. And Ziolda killed one with her Lance of the Sun God. But to Leroy's frustration, no one but Peanut hurried to cover.

Eventually, everyone made it to the cave where the wyverns couldn't reach you, though they remained just outside it until a wall of fire appeared between you and a woman with bright red hair and crimson plate armor dropped down to usher the wyverns back before turning on you.

Session 38

Last time, you met a woman named Midara who got the wyverns attacking you to stand down. She told you that she was the guardian of a dragon egg that was stolen by the cultists along with several wyvern eggs.

You went back to Midara's cave, which had been the lair of the egg's mother. You found out that the cave you previously stayed in had belonged to the egg's father, a moonstone dragon. But that dragon left a very long time ago, as moonstone dragons tend to not stay in one place for long.

Midara asked you to kill a ram for her to cook. She gutted the animal, which made Izzy sick. But luckily for her Flynt was able to conjure some food for them to share. The guts didn't bother Kitt, who took them out to feed the wyverns and get them more friendly toward you. Leroy took the horns from the ram to add to his gauntlet, adding 1d4 of damage for as long as they hold up and don't break off.

After you filled Midara in on your plans hunting the cultists, she said she would come with you, if for nothing else than to get the dragon egg back. You flew from the cave to The Black Pit, following a clue on one of the cultist bodies that read, "Enwin Fizzlethread, Black Pit market, two greens from The Deepwood".

You split up in pairs to search the market. Leroy and Midara went to a shop called "Emotional Support Weaponry" and then a stand called "Eggs" where Leroy threatened Gregg the gnome owner before buying six random eggs.

Ziolda, her cat Zelda, and Kitt in the form of a cat went to a store with furniture such as self-folding chairs and stools with shock absorbers in the legs. Kitt got trapped inside a folding chair, forcing Ziolda to break it to get her out. While Ziolda covered that up and started talking to the owner, Kitt investigated the back room. There was a gnome back there who was crafting the furniture.

You ended up not pursuing that lead and left to investigate other shops first. Eventually, you all met back up with Izzy and Flynt, who were at a clothing shop called Cloak and Swagger. The shop sold clothing made with glammerweave, a magical thread that can create illusions on the fabric when activated.

Leroy commissioned a trench coat with illusory dragon scales. Kitt commissioned a cloak, specifying that she wanted it to create "the spirit of a scary dinosaur with big ol' teeth, but very tasteful". And Ziolda commissioned a collar for Zelda that will create sparkles around her head.

When you went back toward Gregg's egg stand, you found that soldiers had been called there because of Leroy threatening to burn down the stand. Leroy started to pick a fight with the guards and then Midara escalated to killing one of them. Leroy and Midara then quickly fled the city.

While Kitt and Ziolda talked to Gregg and tried to calm things down, Izzy gave Flynt her sending stone to use for a Revivify to bring the guard back.

Kitt then went to investigate the gnome in the back of the furniture store. She talked him into taking her to a secret back room where he had black market goods. She got the eggs, but she also purchased a vial of ghostroot sap, which allows a person to pass through solid objects for one minute, and three unlabeled spellvials, vials made to contain one use of a spell.

You all then decided to wait in town for one of the cultists to arrive to purchase the eggs. During that time, you picked up your commissioned glammerweave items and started determining what spells were contained in the spellvials. You followed the cultist out of town after he failed to get the eggs, and you set an ambush for him. You jumped out and stopped him from activating his suicide amulet and then subdued him, planning to make him lead you to the cultists. The cultist told you that the purpose of creating so many draconians is, in part, to use their lives to resurrect Kymrys.

With the amulet remaining intact, you found the full inscription on it read, "Hail Zaverys the Black, the Dragon Below".

You discussed this new information and connected it to the alternate power sources that the artificers in Cyre had started to use in their more powerful creations instead of Khyber dragonshards. In Kitt's dream about the draconians and the cultists, some of the draconians were wearing Khyber dragonshards around their necks while others wore black shards that seemed to have a faint, flickering, internal fire. These draconians seemed to be less monstrous and corrupted than the ones created using Khyber dragonshards.

This brought Kitt back to her dream while at the Fount of Inarieth, in which she found herself in a dark place, standing on a glassy black surface with veins of glowing red like marble. The presence she felt there had become angry when she impressed the idea of Leroy on it, but it was fine with Kitt and Ziolda.

Ziolda connected with Alderdia and brought this up. Ziolda suggested that this may be another god-dragon, and one with similar arcane power to Khyber, if the black shards can be used in place of the Khyber ones. Alderdia was intrigued by this idea, as the primary problem with his plan to defeat Khyber is how to replace her and her power in the world after she's dead.

Leroy connected with Khyber and asked if she knew anything about another god-dragon named Zaverys and a cult that worshipped them. Khyber was clearly angry and told Leroy to oppose anyone worshipping or following this Zaverys, whoever that may be. Leroy tried to ask more questions and Khyber warned him about insubordination, reminding him that she is his patron, and he has yet to fulfill the task she set him to kill the hermit in The Towering Wood.

While the others were doing this, Kitt was finishing with the spellvials, finding that they contain 7th level spells: Circle of Death, Synaptic Static, and Jallarzi's Storm of Radiance.

Session 39

Last time, you questioned Cragen the cultist and Leroy suggested that Cragen leave the cult and become Ziolda's squire.

Cragen led you to the nearest entrance to the cultists' lair, which he admitted was a back entrance, as the main entrance is far away on the other side of a mountain. You snuck into the tunnel and came upon some strange mushrooms. Ziolda and Leroy threw rocks at one of them. Ziolda hit it and it started shrieking, which set off more mushrooms further down the tunnel.

Cragen just smiled at you setting off the alarm, so Leroy used his bear claws to maul Cragen and make it look like an animal attack. Kitt and Flynt turned into rats to observe the tunnel while everyone else went out to set an ambush in the ravine.

Four cultists and two draconians, a huge bronze one with a Khyber dragonshard around its neck, and a smaller black one with a black pendant on its neck, came to investigate the alarm. Only the black draconian seemed to notice that something was off with the body, but he stayed silent.

Kitt spoke into the bronze draconian's mind and bit its foot to get it to charge after her and Flynt as they ran out of the tunnel. The draconian forced the cultists to run ahead of it as it charged, but the black draconian at the rear took his time.

Once the cultists were out in the open, the ambushers attacked. Ziolda attacked the black draconian, who cast Dominate Person on her and held up his hands to get everyone to stop fighting after the cultists were killed. He said his name is Drakul, and he wants to be free of the cultists, as do others who have the black shards on their necks.

These draconians call themselves The Enlightened, or The True Children of the Dragons. They had three leaders, gold draconians who were hatched from the same egg. You killed one of them in the ruins of the Solamnic fort while another teleported the cultist there away. He and the other remaining brother are still here.

The Enlightened are pressed into service by the cultists who created them. The draconians with Khyber dragonshards are as well, but they like the killing and violence.

You informed Drakul of the cultists' intentions to sacrifice the draconians to resurrect the dragon Kymrys, and Kitt inspected the pendant on Drakul's neck. The pendant was magically bound to Drakul, but Kitt was able to remove it by casting Dispel Magic on it.

Session 40

Last time, you infiltrated the cult, Drakul introduced you to the leader of the Enlightened, Therak, and you split up to check the place out and accomplish a few goals.

Midara, with the draconian Drakul and bat-Kitt, went to find the dragon and wyvern eggs. In addition to the red one that Midara was trying to retrieve, you also found a silver one and a white one. Kitt had to talk Midara out of immediately killing everyone around them to get the egg.

Ziolda, Therak, and Izzy went to the cult's prison to free Volden, Therak's brother. You found that he was being held by a Mental Prison spell that was being cast from a large black stone, like the ones the Enlightened draconians have around their necks. Izzy pried the stone from the ceiling and Ziolda saw that the flame inside it was drawn slightly toward her hands when she was holding it. She used Divine Sense to sense good or evil from it. What she sensed was the potential for either, but the presence of neither.

Leroy, with two salamanders, Flynt and Peanut, in his pockets, went to investigate the tunnel where the draconians were not allowed to go. He found that cultists would line up to pass through a curtain one at a time into a small room. When it came to his turn in the room, he saw that it contained a stone tablet covered in runes and a cushion that appeared to be for kneeling in prayer. He made a copy of the tablet before leaving the room.

Leroy dropped Flynt and Peanut off with Ziolda and then went to the ritual chamber where the cultists hatched draconians. Flynt examined the black stone and the flame inside it immediately shot to the edges and burned his hands, causing him to drop it. Ziolda thought about the way Khyber dragonshards store magic and how these stones were used to power the warforged colossi in place of Khyber dragonshards. She also thought about how Eberron dragonshards could be used to cast sustained, low-level spells. This stone seemed to be operating in a similar way.

Leroy went to the ritual chamber where five cultists were carving runes into a human skeleton that he reasoned belonged to the ancient paladin Khardam.

You coordinated to have everyone go loud at once. Ziolda was to break the black stone to try to cause an explosion. Oswald was let loose with a huge pipe bomb to definitely cause an explosion. Therak would try to warn as many of the Enlightened as possible to stay away from the ritual chamber. And Midara and Drakul would grab the dragon and wyvern eggs before everyone met back up in the ritual chamber.

Ziolda hit the stone multiple times, even smiting it, but couldn't break it. In the end, she decided to risk harming Volden and take the stone with her from the room. The Mental Prison spell triggered and nearly killed him, but he survived.

Midara grabbed her egg and let loose a blast of fire that completely incinerated all of the cultists in front of her, revealing herself to be a red dragon who had gotten stuck in a polymorphed elf form after her egg was stolen. She stayed there to close down one avenue the cultists could take to reach the ritual chamber.

Oswald threw his pipe bomb, which shook the whole tunnel system but didn't quite cause it to collapse. It did kill a lot of cultists, though.

Leroy used the chaos of the bomb to try to take the skull from the skeleton, but when he touched the skull, it triggered another Mental Prison spell. He managed to escape the spell but took damage from it as he shadow stepped away. The skull could not be removed from the plinth, but Leroy did succeed in punching holes in the skull where he tried to grab it, disrupting some of the runes.

Khyber draconians started rushing toward the explosion, most of the Enlightened having been warned to stay away. But when they entered the room, the cultists triggered their spells on the skeleton. The runes started glowing and all of the draconians in the room exploded. Their blood rushed to the plinth to start forming a body that rose from the plinth. Ziolda recognized him as a twisted version of Khardam Silvereye.

Session 41

Last time, you squared off against the cultists of The Dragon Below and the spirit of the ancient dragon Kymrys inhabiting the resurrected body of Khardam Silvereye. Kymrys spotted Ziolda and recognized the aura of one of Siberys's paladins. He asked how many times he had to have this fight and said that you can't kill what the Great Dragon has blessed.

During the fight, Kymrys stabbed Ziolda many times, intent on bringing her down before attacking anyone else. Eventually, he conjured a hallucinatory mist that paralyzed everyone with fear as they saw the same events playing out in different ways.

Leroy saw Kymrys turn into the first werewolf and then into his son in werewolf form. Arel attacked Kitt and then Ziolda, though Leroy saw them as his daughter and wife. He also saw Lord Soth lurking in the background, observing the fight.

Kitt saw Kymrys turn into the old man who killed her parents, standing over their bodies and the body of Weyla. She also saw Peanut's face starting to rot away and her own hands start to wither in the old man's presence. He attacked her and she blacked out as the feeling of poison flowed through her.

Ziolda saw Kymrys turn into first Traelor, and then a version of herself. The other Ziolda said that the only way to beat Khyber is to embrace Khyber's magic as Traelor did. She said that the party is already on the way there. The hilt of Ziolda's glaive contains the power of lightning originally imbued in Inarieth's sword by the storm elemental she fought. Leroy made a deal with Khyber and accepted her power, and it has saved the party multiple times. And Kitt has been dabbling in necromancy. The other Ziolda said that undead are Khyber's twisted mockery of the living and asked what that makes Ziolda.

When Kymrys attacked Ziolda, he pulled off her pendant, removing her soul from her body, which fell to the ground dead again.

Inside the pendant, Ziolda managed to hold herself and the crystal around her together as it started to shatter. With Khardam's help, she managed to get into his realm where he healed her and told her that they could get Kymrys out of it forever if they could find his helm.

Ziolda used Locate Object to find the helm before Kymrys could attack, potentially saving you all from a total party kill. She retrieved the helm and Khardam used it to cast Holy Weapon on his pike so Ziolda could use it to drive Kymrys out of the realm for good.

Khardam gave her his helm, which gave Ziolda her Aura of Courage. This immediately broke Kitt and Leroy out of their paralysis and fear, though they had to get the pendant back to Ziolda to get her spirit back into her body. Leroy and Flynt teamed up to get Kymrys to drop it, and Leroy tossed it to Kitt to put on Ziolda.

Kymrys started killing the surrounding draconians to heal himself and used his Cataclysmic Breath twice. The second time nearly killed Ziolda and Leroy. It did kill Izzy, who rose up as a zombie under Kymrys's control. Kitt, on the other hand was saved by her belt, which turned out to be cursed. Her body exploded and she emerged as a wolf.

Leroy and Ziolda finished off Kymrys and the fight between draconians resolved, with the Enlightened emerging victorious. Midara rejoined you and cremated Khardam's bones while you tried to figure out what to do about Izzy and Kitt. Kitt reasoned that if Flynt removed the curse and Kitt dropped to 0 hit points again, she might change back. Despite starting to enjoy being a wolf and getting head scratches from Leroy, Kitt asked him to attack her. Leroy did, and Kitt's wolf body exploded, turning her back into a halfling.

After you translated the text Leroy copied from the cultists, you deduced that Zaverys, The Dragon Below, is the son of Eberron and Siberys, and the cultists were confusing him with Khyber, The Great Dragon.

The crystal Ziolda took from the cultist's prison was a Zaverys dragonshard, and after Flynt's Revivify spell failed to bring Izzy back, you reasoned that if Zaverys is the anti-Khyber, then the Zaverys dragonshard might work. It had reacted strongly to Flynt, and you thought that he might be a champion of Zaverys in the same way you're all champions of a Divine Dragon. Flynt brought Izzy back, but she's now blind and one hand is still withered.

Izzy told you she can't adventure with you anymore after what she's been through. She wanted to return to Sharn and wanted Flynt to come with her. He had promised Leroy that he would help with Arel in Cyre, and he ultimately decided that he will go with you on your expedition into The Mournlands after Izzy is safely back home in Sharn.

Kitt looked at Izzy's withered hand and thought about how she used necromancy to do the same thing to several cultists. She resolved to try to learn to use her natural talent for necromancy in more wholesome ways, something the wizards at Arcanix could help with.

Midara gave Leroy a boon as thanks for helping get her egg back and in hopes of helping him get his son back. She also gave some power to Peanut, who grew to normal fastieth size, so Kitt can always ride him. He also has red stripes, horns, and an internal heat that never goes away.

Ziolda solidified her connection to Khardam and he gave her the ability to inspire those around her as a Knight of the Rose, letting her dole out a lot of temp hit points.

Interlude 7

Session 42

Last time, you traveled north from the Blackcaps to take care of a few things before escorting Izzy to Sharn.

Kitt went to Arcanix with Flynt and Izzy, where she planned to speak with the necromancers about using her necromantic talents in a more wholesome way. Professor Zalyr told her that necromancy is about manipulating the forces of both life and death, but healing someone with necromancy requires a sacrifice to move that energy around. She gave Kitt information about spells to do this.

Meanwhile, Flynt and Kitt's attempts to heal Izzy's hand failed, leading Flynt to conclude that the tissue may have died and was therefore impossible to restore with the Revivify spell. At this point, Greater Restoration just staves off further decay. Flynt resolved to learn to make scrolls of Greater Restoration that will help Izzy until you find a solution to the problem or until she accepts that she may need to have it amputated.

Leroy and Ziolda went up to the Crimson Knights to touch base about the upcoming expedition into the Mournlands. While walking through the surrounding forest, you found mysterious tracks that led off the path and then stopped at a small pool of blood. It appeared someone was watching the blood hunters' keep, and Leroy found an impression in the dirt that appeared to be some sort of sigil.

You reported this to the blood hunter leaders, who told you that Orlan was currently tracking down a lead on the possible blood hunter traitors. He was leading a small band of blood hunters and asked you to delay your expedition until he can join you. While you spoke with them, a signal from the gates alerted you to someone approaching.

The man outside the gates was Ronin, the blood hunter Leroy convinced to go into Cyre when the Mourning happened. But the mists had changed him. His skin is nearly translucent, and his hair is stark white. His blood hunter armor was gone and the rags of his clothes held together with bronze webs. Leroy smelled a lingering scent of ozone on him and Ziolda's glaive crackled with static near him. He confirmed Leroy's theory that the mists affected him after the explosion of the Khyber dragonshard that powered the crashing airship.

Ronin told you he had been captured and worked as a slave in what the warforged call The Forge-Sworn Empire. He was forced to work in the mines, digging for obsidian dragonshards, for three years until he came to grips with his power and broke out. He spent the last year surveying the land to bring the blood hunters useful information.

Kitt took a test with the heads of all three branches of the Mages of High Sorcery. She was thrown into an illusion of Gatherhold being attacked by a huge earth elemental. Faced with saving an injured halfling, stopping the collapse of a building onto another, and facing off against the elemental chasing a third, she chose to save the one that was already injured.

She was then pulled into an illusion of a ship in a storm, being attacked by a kraken. Again, she chose to heal the sailor whose back had been broken by the kraken. When she emerged from the illusion, Professor Arlo Ikalis told her that her instincts show she belongs with the Mages of the White Robes just as much as with the Black Robes. He worked with Kitt to use her necromancy to cast traditionally abjuration spells.

Ziolda and Leroy went to Flamekeep and were immediately met with a crowd gathered around a man speaking about the heretics and heathens in Karrnath and New Cyre. He spoke of how Cardinal Halidor and Queen Diani have failed Thrane and how Cardinal Bedan is ready to begin a new crusade. Ziolda and Leroy were ready to fight him but decided to go to the queen in peace.

Queen Diani explained the current political situation in Thrane and said that there are essentially two coalitions on the Council, with Cardinal Aldami as the swing vote. She's worried that Aldami might be swayed to Bedan's side, if not by words, then possibly by threats. Ziolda said she would see what she could do to convince Aldami to stay on Diani's side.

Ziolda then filled Queen Diani in on what you've learned about the Divine Dragons and your mission to defeat Khyber. She also said that she would like to be publicly put out there as a champion of The Silver Flame in the mold of Tira, in an effort to combat the rhetoric of Cardinal Bedan's supporters.

Queen Diani got you access to restricted archives where Leroy found first-hand depictions of the battle between Tira and The Great Demon. The images made The Great Demon appear like an oversized orc, and one of the creatures his forces rode on resembled the mage hunter you fought in the fighting pits of Droaam.

Ziolda met with Cardinal Aldami and found out that she believed she and her family were being followed, and that if she didn’t side with Bedan’s position, her family would be in danger. Ziolda promised to get the queen to assign loyal soldiers to protect her and her family.

Back at Arcanix, Flynt was discussing what he learned about copper dragons. He said that the green patina effect on the egg was observed on the scales of older copper dragons, particularly those who lived in the salty air near the sea. While you discussed this, Peanut reacted to the copper egg hatching in his saddlebag. The wyrmling peeked its head out and curiously looked around at the halflings.

After accidentally burning Izzy with the acid on its tongue, the dragon started to warm up to her. Flynt pointed out that the bright blue of its eyes meant it was a female, and Izzy suggested the nickname Penny. The halflings got ready to meet back up with Leroy and Ziolda to take the lightning rail to Sharn.

Session 43

Last time, you entered Sharn, The City of Towers, with Izzy and Flynt, and made your way to House Ghallanda in the upper reaches of the city. Along the way, you took note of some bounty posters being put up and security forces blocking off passage to the Undercity. A House Deneith officer said there was a very recent attack by people from the Undercity, part of an emerging pattern. She told you where you could find more information about the culprits, some of whose faces were on the bounty posters.

You reached Izzy's home and met her mother, Fayda, who gave Kitt a small look, as if seeing a little bit of Kitt's father there. Kitt confirmed this by telling Fayda that she's Elwin's daughter. Fayda's expression told you that she knew what that meant for a dragonmark, but she kept that, and Kitt's full identity, from the rest of her family.

She offered you all a place to stay as long as you're in Sharn, and you headed to the markets to browse their magical items. While there, you heard that someone recently stole items that you took to be spell components of some kind.

You went to the market that the victims were headed toward when they were attacked. You suspected smuggling of some kind and Leroy intimidated the clerk into revealing that he was smuggling. He was just smuggling weed into work and stashing it so he could smoke it when he was supposed to be working, in violation of company policy.

You investigated the boxes on the cart that was attacked, and Peanut found a strange residue in the bottom that you presumed was drugs. Ziolda was reminded of the Thrane deserter you saw at The Great Crag, who was buying a drug known as azurium from a gnomish dealer.

You got more information from the security post the Deneith officer directed you toward, and you saw three would-be bounty hunters getting ready to go after the people on the wanted posters as well. Ziolda saw new posters being put up and recognized one of them as her old captain, Keladry.

You followed the bounty hunters into the Undercity and saw them get immediately eviscerated by monstrous creatures. You ran forward and killed the monsters but were too late for the bounty hunters. Staying stealthier, you avoided more of them as you searched the city and eventually came across a small encampment.

Kel and three of the other bounties were there, all clearly very high on the azurium, and one was overdosing, actively transforming into one of the monsters. Leroy stepped in and killed him mid-transformation and Kitt and Ziolda teamed up to stop the other two from overdosing and cleared Kel's head enough for her to talk with you.

Kel told you that they had been compelled to attack the shipment. The azurium dealers had vanished from the Undercity and this stuff was more potent. It seemed that someone was trying to force people to overdose. In the attack, Kel had ripped out an earring from a halfling who was with the shipment. She showed it to you, and you saw that it had the letters BC on it.

Ziolda and Leroy debated what to do with Kel, and you landed on taking all three of them back to the security checkpoint to collect the bounties, but Ziolda insisted on Kel being then taken to House Deneith so Kel's family could take care of her.

The dwarf at the security office pointed you to the Boromar Clan, who had been sponsoring the bounties to make it seem like they were the only ones willing to do something about cleaning up the crime in Sharn and increase their political standing. He told you that they're a sort of splinter group that claims ties to House Jorasco, but they don't have dragonmarks.

The other bounty you collected woke up at the talk about dragonmarks and saw the one on the dwarf. In his brief moment of lucidity, he tried to communicate that the people they attacked also had dragonmarks, but they looked wrong, sort of infected.

You went to the Boromar Clan headquarters and Kitt attempted to sneak around as a cat, but it appeared to just be a boring business building. She returned to the group, and you went in the front door. The receptionist quickly realized that you were on to the operation, and she triggered a silent alarm that locked the doors and alerted assassins, each with an aberrant dragonmark, to come attack you.

After you defeated them, you made your way up the building until you found the leaders who were behind all of this, a relatively high member of the Boromar Clan and a member of House Tarkanan, which is a loose association of people with aberrant dragonmarks, similar to the structure of House Vol but lesser in strength and influence.

You turned them in and informed the other dragonmarked houses in Sharn of their plot. Together, they made plans to clean up the Undercity, killing any monsters and bringing the azuium addicts to centers that the two halfling houses would set up to help them.

Then you returned to House Ghallanda to rest and consider what you would buy with your earnings.

Session 44

Last time, you prepared to leave Sharn to deal with Khyber's command to Leroy to kill a hermit living in the forests of the Eldeen Reaches. Leroy must do this in return for Khyber saving you from petrification when you fought The Lord of Dust on Draksholm.

Before you left, Leroy was visited by a ghostly version of Brian, the last person he killed on Khyber's command. One of Leroy's eggs also hatched, revealing a snakelike creature with chameleonlike camouflage known as a jaculi, who Leroy named Stuart.

You left Sharn and skirted around the village of Greenblade to avoid any confrontation at the tavern where Oswald stole the Greenblade that Kitt now has.

When you entered the forest near the hermit's abode, you came across a site that looked like it had once been used for mining quartz, but it was eroded and smooth from years of weather and disuse.

You then came across poorly tended copse of trees and bushes giving off a sweet smell. You found that the thorny plants had sour berries, and Kitt identified them as blackthorn, which is sometimes used by druids to make staffs.

You continued to a similarly poorly tended garden that contained a mix of normal herbs and spices and plants used for making potions. Kitt gathered materials for some strong healing potions.

When you reached the hermits house, you found it to be a simple, rectangular, single-story building. But attached to the back was a slightly larger, domed building. When you went to the door, you found a nice old man who introduced himself as Styarn. He offered you biscuits with blackthorn jam and tea made with herbs from his garden.

While Leroy searched for any way to justify killing Styarn, he saw the specter of Brian move past one of the windows.

Styarn told you about how he had studied at Arcanix long ago but moved out to the forest to study the stars in peace, away from people, lights, airships, and the war. He cleared out an area of the forest and built his house and observatory. When he was more fit, he would mine the quartz to make the lenses for his telescopes.

He asked you not to go into his observatory because there were many delicate instruments in there that had to be carefully aligned to track objects in the sky. But when you looked in there, you found that many of them were damaged. Styarn said he can't remember what could have caused that and supposed that as he's gotten older, he's had a few more accidents just doing his everyday activities.

You looked at the room more closely and determined that there seemed to have been some sort of explosion in the middle of the room. Leroy found a small hole in the wall by the door. Inside it was a tiny glimmer of green. After that, you noticed that there were many of these tiny holes around the walls of the room.

You checked Styarn and found that he had small marks on him like the ones on the walls, like scars. Kitt also felt that his pulse was strange, almost like too much volume with each pulse and moving slower than would be expected.

While Ziolda stayed with Styarn, Kitt and Leroy went back to thoroughly examine every part of the room. Kitt found cabinet that contained a green crystal suspended in midair. She immediately tried to swipe it and when she made contact, it exploded.

Shards of the crystal went into Kitt's thigh, embedding themselves in her femur, and into Leroy's chest, surrounding his heart. Kitt can feel them whenever she takes a step, and Leroy can feel them poking at his heart every time it beats.

Kitt's speed increased by 5 feet, but she will take an extra 1d6 falling damage from now on. Leroy can now cast Haste on himself at will, but when it ends, he gains a level of exhaustion that can only be removed by a long rest.

Session 45

Last time, you dealt with the aftermath of the exploding crystal in the lab of the hermit wizard Styarn. Leroy felt his formerly broken back start to hurt and his feet go numb as Khyber sent a message to get on with the job.

Leroy cut Styarn's throat and he exploded into black goo. It seemed to be resistant to almost everything you hit it with until it finally got hit by cold damage and Ziolda crit with Lance of the Sun God and a Thunderous Smite to deal 132 damage on a single hit, killing it.

You worked out that the green crystal may have been the result of Styarn, a wizard and an astromancer, attempting to synthesize a new dragonshard by combining Khyber's arcane magic with Siberys's starlight. Unaware of the true nature of the magic and the gods, when Styarn combined them, it made the crystal very unstable, resulting in an explosion of anomalous magic. You reasoned that something similar may have happened in Cyre, but on a much larger scale.

You returned to Sharn to get Flynt and then went to Cardell Keep to gather the Crimson Knights who would be joining you in the Mournlands. Leroy decided that of the three leaders, Istar would be the best to bring because of his experience with mutagens. Two more Order of the Mutant blood hunters, Rob and Fenlin, joined, along with Desi the warforged, Gharok the goliath werebear, Tallara from the Order of the Ghostslayer, and Orlan, the leader of the Crimson Knights.

You made for Flamekeep with this crew and Ziolda found that there are now posters of her around the city, touting her as the second coming of Tira Miron, and she was getting recognized all over. When you made for the docks, you were met by three Thrane soldiers, Viktor, Nia, and Ellis, who were selected by Queen Diani to join your mission.

You set out along the river and entered the mists near where Ziolda fought the warforged colossus years ago.
