The Chronicle · Arc 2

Arc 2 — Unrest in Thaliost

Sessions 5–10

Session 5 Session 6 Session 7 Session 8 Interlude 2 Session 9 Session 10

Session 5

Last time, you headed back to Flamekeep to get information about the Church of the Silver Flame's dealings with werewolves over the last few centuries. On the way, you stopped again at the Face of Tira. Ziolda had a dream about her old captain and mentor, Kel, surviving on the day of the Mourning. Kitt had a dream where she was searching for Peanut, who had gone missing. Then, the powder of a crushed blue crystal hissed at her before she grabbed her staff and a roar from behind her dispersed the floating powder. And Leroy dreamed of Lakeside and Lake Dark, where his daughter and wife spoke to him from deep within the lake, telling him that they would not be reunited yet, that Khorvaire still needed him.

When you got to Flamekeep, Leroy and Ziolda went to the Church's Hall of Records, but they found that Ziolda had been refused access by High Priest Gloz and the Council of Cardinals. Leroy, disguised as her assistant with makeup expertly applied by Peanut, bribed the two guards with 70 gold pieces to gain access. Ziolda chose to stay outside so the guards could honestly say they didn't let her in.

Inside, Leroy made copies of information on Kel and Alianne, Ziolda's old squadmate, the Lycan Crusade, and the secret organization called the Silver Moon: rangers dedicated to hunting werewolves across western Khorvaire. Essentially, they are the Church's equivalent of the Crimson Knights.

Then you went back to Thaliost, refusing to stop at the Face of Tira again. In Thaliost, you met Governor Rask, who was arguing with Captain Wesker about the best way to deal with the Dragonhawks. Wesker wanted to answer their attacks with equally deadly force, but the governor said that martyrdom would only increase their numbers and escalate things further.

When a building exploded outside with almost uncanny timing, you all, along with Sergeant Kiri Bluehelm and Kasimir d'Deneith, a hired mercenary, rushed to help. The governor asked you to avoid killing any Dragonhawks. When you arrived, soldiers and Dragonhawks were fighting in the streets and more Dragonhawks were shooting from hiding places on and in nearby buildings.

Leroy rushed into the burning Gold Dragon Inn and Tavern and managed to save all four people trapped in there. Kitt climbed the building to get a vantage point on the archers, prepping to track them to their hideout. Ziolda fought alongside Kiri and Kasimir in the street. And Peanut helped the fire brigade put out the fire before it could spread. In the fighting, two soldiers and one Dragonhawk were killed, the other six Dragonhawk thugs were captured, and the archers took off running.

Kitt transformed into a giant wolf spider to chase her archer while Leroy teleported onto a rooftop to catch sight of another. Ziolda threw him a Dragonhawk mask, and he started off after his archer while Ziolda stayed in the street to deal with the tied-up thugs.

Session 6

Last time, we picked up with Kitt chasing a Dragonhawk as a giant spider, and Leroy chasing a Dragonhawk across the rooftops and through the rubble of the Reconstruction, while Ziolda took care of the Dragonhawk prisoners and Peanut received 150 gold for his heroics with the fire brigade.

Kitt snuck into a safehouse after the Dragonhawk and found he had small amounts of chemicals for bombs stronger than what was used on the tavern. After knocking out the Dragonhawk and searching through his hidden cache, she found a cracked sending stone, a map of the safehouses and three entrances to the Dragonhawks' hideouts, and a drawing of a circle with an intricate woven pattern and a blue stone in the middle.

Leroy just managed to keep up with his Dragonhawk and found that she was a half-elven woman who didn't seem too enthusiastic about the day's mission. He convinced her he was a Dragonhawk named Jerry long enough for her to let him into the safehouse, but when he took off the mask, she realized he wasn't actually Jerry. Through discussion with Ëarwyn, Leroy found out about someone named Lianna, who had recently come to the Dragonhawks and spurred them to more violent actions. She also seemed to have some sort of hold over the Dragonhawks' leader, Beren.

Ëarwyn agreed to go with Leroy to help stop Lianna's faction of extremist Dragonhawks, so long as you avoided killing the older Dragonhawks who were her friends. She also remembered that there was an ambush planned in the eventuality that prisoners were taken.

Ziolda fell into the ambush, led by a half-orc named Kuragash, on the way to Fort Thaliost. After a Dragonhawk throwing bombs killed one soldier and nearly killed Kiri, Ziolda summoned luminous, golden wings and flew up to the rooftop to take him out. Below, Kitt, Leroy, and Ëarwyn arrived at the battle and Kitt discovered that Peanut was missing. After everyone struggled to take down Kuragash, Kitt yelled, "Where's my dinosaur!" and cast heat metal on him, at which point he turned and fled through the flames that blocked the street, disappearing into the shadows.

You regrouped at Fort Thaliost to confront the Governor and Captain Wesker, for some reason, questioning them instead of the prisoners. You decided on a plan to send soldiers to the Dragonhawk safehouses and the entrances marked on Kitt's map. Kiri and Kasimir would each lead a group, and you gave Kiri Ëarwyn's speaking stone.

Leroy sent a letter by raven to Bervodd asking about Lianna's possible connection to the Blood of Vol. Kitt tried to write "Missing dinosaur, RIP" but Leroy crossed it out.

Then Kitt sent a message to Flynt through her sending stone that said: "It's Kitt. In Thaliost. Rebel group, Dragonhawks, experimenting with incendiary, percussive, corrosive bombs. Led by Lianna. Have any info? Also Peanut is missing. Luv, Kitt."

Session 7

Last time, you had your longest day ever as, after multiple battles and chases, you tried to track down the Dragonhawk leaders and rescue Peanut. After Ziolda pulled rank on Captain Wesker, reminding him that she spoke for the Crown, she won the soldiers over to your side. You ordered that the city be locked down and sent teams of soldiers to each of the Dragonhawk locations marked on the map Kitt discovered.

Before leaving, however, you questioned the prisoners and found that they were willing to die for their cause, believing themselves immortal. Ziolda used her divine sense to tell that, though they were neither demons nor undead, there was something infernal about them. Something about their eyes hinted that they were much older than they appeared, and this reminded Kitt of rumors among her tribe that evil magics kept the people of Q'barra, to the south of the Talenta Plains, alive far longer than was natural.

When you split up the Thaliost forces, Kiri led one group, Kasimir led another, and you led the last one, with Ëarwyn, along the path most likely to get you to the main hideout. After passing through traps in the catacombs, you came to the headquarters and found it had been cleared out. But Kuragash was waiting there. He had warned the Dragonhawks that Ëarwyn was a traitor and they had left, taking any hints toward their plans with them.

In discussion with Kuragash, you found that he was honor- and duty-bound to the job for which he was hired, which was to aid the Dragonhawks under Beren's instructions. However, you convinced him that Lianna was warping Beren's mind and that his duty required him to free Beren and show you where Lianna was hiding.

Kuragash led you to where Lianna seemed to be preparing a ritual, with Peanut chained down on a sort of altar in an old crypt. She produced a pendant with a blue crystal that shattered and summoned a blue, dragonlike demon. In the fight that ensued, Lianna was taken down immediately by spider Kitt, who jabbed Lianna with her stinger. But the demon didn't disappear, and it awakened several ghoulish beings from the crypt that attacked the group.

While the soldiers broke Peanut free, Kitt took quite a beating, getting knocked out of her spider form and nearly knocked unconscious. Ziolda took on the demon with Kuragash and Leroy, who transformed into his werebear form for the first time in combat. When the demon, realizing victory was impossible, tried to get at least one kill by attacking Kitt, Kitt remembered her dream. As she cast a Shatter spell at the demon, a golden light burst from her staff and she also cast a 3rd level Spirit Shroud spell. In doing so, her neck seared again, this time more sharply, but she was able to weaken the demon enough for Leroy to get the final blow in.

As the demon died, the ghouls deanimated and you were free to get everyone who had survived out of the catacombs. Beren, free of whatever power Lianna had over him, was extremely exhausted and unresponsive to anything you asked him. Lianna, still unconscious, was locked up in the fort while you went to speak with the governor.

You made plans to get some much-needed rest--Leroy trancing on the battlement over the main entrance while the rest stayed inside the fort and waited for Kiri and Kasimir to lead their squads back. As you went to bed, hoping no bombs would go off in the meantime, Kitt got three messages on her speaking stone from Flynt, telling her that he was too far away to be of much real help, but that her priority should be figuring out what the target of each type of bomb could be. You also noticed that Captain Wesker was nowhere to be found in the fort.

Session 8

Last time, you took a rest, hoping that Lianna's bombs wouldn't go off during the night. Thankfully, they didn't. When Kiri and Kasimir led what was left of their soldiers back to the fort, the clues they found led Leroy to believe that Raven's Rise would be a target for one of the bombs. He and Kasimir went to Raven's Rise and searched it thoroughly, repeatedly waking up a very sleepy, very unhelpful representative of House Lyrandar. But they didn't find any bombs.

Meanwhile, Ziolda interrogated Lianna, who told her that she had been sent by Lord Soth, from near Madstone in Karrnath, to cause chaos in Thaliost and help "prepare the way for the Heir of the Blood." She saw the silver rings in Ziolda's eyes and told her that she knew the true nature of the Silver Flame and the Blood of Vol, and that she knew what Ziolda really is because she's seen the real prophecy. She told Ziolda two versions of this prophecy, one held by the Church of the Silver Flame and the other by the Blood of Vol. While Ziolda was processing the prophecy, Lianna cast Sword Burst on herself before Ziolda could stop her, killing herself before she could give anything else away.

Kitt received a message from Flynt detailing how he thought each type of bomb might be triggered. She wild shaped into a bloodhound to try to sniff out the bombs and everyone ended up at Raven's Rise.

After some discussion about the targets of the bombs, the party split up. Ziolda took Earwyn to the bridge, Leroy and Kitt took Kasimir to the airship docks, and Kiri was left to guard the fort where the governor was staying.

At an auction house, a ship owned by a dwarf merchant named Hulrik was being unloaded, and Kitt caught the scent of the corrosive bomb. Everyone there ran for the ship as it took off into the sky, being hijacked by Dragonhawks.

On the bridge, Ziolda saw the ship take off with everyone hanging from it in one big chain. She inspected the construction equipment on the bridge before she saw workers running for the train. While the soldiers occupied most of the Dragonhawks, Ziolda jumped into the cab of the train and pulled the brakes. But unfortunately, that's where the bomb was hidden, and it fell out onto the tracks. Earwyn tried to shoot it off the rails with arrows, but the train ran over it, and it detonated, killing a lot of people who were fleeing from the scene and launching the train into the water. Ziolda managed to get out of the train before it landed, activating her radiant wings and flying back to the bridge as smoke started rising from the fort.

On the airship, Kasimir and Leroy took on the Dragonhawks as Kitt and Peanut ran for the bomb. With a Thunderwave spell, Kitt hurt the pilot but also badly damaged the steering mechanism of the ship. She managed to take the bomb from the pilot's bag of holding and got it to Kasimir, who neutralized it while Leroy finished off the Dragonhawks. Seeing the smoke over the fort, you piloted the ship toward it and found Kiri had managed to put up a strong defense and kept the governor safe.

You discussed how to deal with the Dragonhawks and set Thaliost up for the future to keep everything calm. Beren, the leader of the Dragonhawks is to be jailed after publicly telling any Dragonhawks to stand down and disband. Earwyn is to take up a new role as a sort of liaison to the people of the Reconstruction and Governor Rask promised to ensure funding is, at the very least, split between the Reconstruction and the bridge.

When you returned the ship to Hulrik, he offered you one of his magic items that were to be sold at auction, and you chose a Rope of Climbing. You also got the Bag of Holding from the Dragonhawk. Hulrik said he needs to go back to the Mror Holds to get his ship fixed and if you're going in that direction to Lakeside, he can take you with him.

But first, Ziolda wanted to go back to Flamekeep and Leroy wanted to go back to Cardell Keep, bringing Kitt with him. Hulrik said he could wait a few days for you to get back.

Interlude 2

Session 9

Last time, you left Thaliost and split up, Leroy and Kitt going to the Crimson Knights and Ziolda reporting to Queen Diani. On the way to Cardell Keep, Leroy and Kitt encountered one of the captains of the Silver Moon, Kezia Coldward, who was investigating rumors of secretive people sneaking around in the Silver Wood.

Leroy proceeded to punch her in the face, which resulted in her hidden archers shooting him and her slashing him with her sword before he realized she had a Silver Moon tattoo behind her ear. Leroy managed to talk down the rangers and told them that they had a common goal. He promised someone from his order would come explain more to them if they let Leroy and Kitt continue to the hidden keep.

In Cardell Keep, Bervodd taught Leroy some orcish signals to let Moridar, an undercover Silver Moon captain in Droaam, know that they were friends and want to speak to him in private. Meanwhile, Kitt had a telepathic conversation with Vitara Shei, who informed Kitt that she had never encountered a halfling with telepathy. Kitt realized that Ghostwise Halflings are not a real thing and that Weyla had made up the term to explain Kitt's strange powers. You also put together that Kitt's staff and the burning at the back of her neck are connected, and a small burn-like mark appears when the staff is close to it.

Meanwhile, in Flamekeep, Ziolda met up with Siu, who asked why Ziolda ignored her advice to keep her amnesia quiet. Now the Council of Cardinals is wary of her, believing whatever caused the mists in the Mournlands could have some latent effect on Ziolda's mind. Ziolda described the prophecy she had received from Lianna, including the line "Reclaimed from Death's fell grip," which led her to ask if she's even alive. Finding no pulse in her wrist, she realized she's in some sort of undead, but not zombified, state.

She then reported what happened in Thaliost to the queen, leaving out the details of her undeath. Diani believes that Cardinal Halidor and Cardinal Aldami could be won over to their side when it comes to funding and preventing another war. Cardinal Raren would be heavily against them, and Cardinals Siao and Bedan would take some tough convincing.

When Diani mentioned Cardinal Halidor, Ziolda mentioned that she'd like an audience with him alone, away from the other cardinals. Diani set that up and Ziolda asked about his resurrection. He recalled a shadow appearing and coalescing into a man, who put a hand to Halidor's chest, stopping his heart. The next thing he knew was being resurrected by Jaela. Ziolda said her questions were because she had run across information about the Blood of Vol and their apparent leader, Lord Soth. But she stopped short of disclosing her undeath to him.

The party then met back up and traveled with Hulrik to Lakeside, where Istar and Sylvari were investigating Lake Dark and the Blood of Vol's interest in it. Sylvari identified Kitt's staff as being a dragon wing bone and she and Istar presented their research into the diving competition that takes place every year in Lake Dark. Everyone dove down as deeply as they could, with Leroy taking a potion, Kitt wildshaping, and Ziolda not needing to breathe. Ziolda felt a pull of belonging as she got deeper, beckoning her to keep going down. But eventually, the eerie cold overcame everyone, and you all had to surface.

Istar suggested that the Cyre emblems on the hilts used to control the zombies could mean that it might not have been the Blood of Vol after all. Then four figures attacked you. A mage tossed a totem into the water, freezing it before causing it to erupt in chaos of icy spirit energy. You defeated the attackers and knocked out the mage. In the process, Kitt found her Spirit Shroud was supercharged by the lake and Leroy got the sense that a hand he felt on his shoulder, reassuring him, belonged to his wife.

After the fight, you unmasked the dead attackers and found it was Gaius and Aelbard, the knights of the Order of the Emerald Claw you helped in the Nightwood weeks ago. You put together that this lake has some sort of connection to the spirit realm. A sort of veil between the worlds that's thin at the bottom of the lake. The mage, Tiergon, said that all he knew was that the Order is interested in this lake for that reason, and that they're trying to figure out what happened to Cyre and how to reverse it. Ziolda made a pact with Tiergon that he will become your spy within the Order and inform her if they show interest in any more places like this in Khorvaire, sealing the pact with a brand from her pendant.

Knowing you only have two nights before the full moon, you made plans to visit Weyla in the Talenta Plains and Flynt in New Cyre on your way to Droaam. But only after Leroy deals with his full moon transformation.

Session 10

Last time, you had a couple days in Lakeside before Leroy's first full moon since becoming a werebear.

Kitt learned from Sylvari how to make a bunch of new potions, provided she can find the ingredients.

Leroy went down to Lake Dark to commune with the spirits of his wife and daughter, who offered him reassurance and told him not to lose himself for their sakes. Leroy said he had found himself in his quest for revenge, then he prepared for his transformation.

Ziolda spent some time meditating on the Silver Flame, partially to find answers and partially to help ignore a tied-up Leroy asking for salmon from his bag. She was hoping to find answers about her undeath, and she remembered the vision of someone crawling toward her across a battlefield, but this time it was accompanied by the scent of the incense Alianne used to burn when praying to the Silver Flame.

When the full moon rose, Leroy found himself, in werebear form, on a black, glassy plateau. He reached for his salmon bag and was attacked by a swarm of angry, biting salmon. Then he was attacked by three werewolves. While fighting them, he realized he had his Hellish Rebuke scroll and cast it. Everyone watching him saw him cast it in real life too. Then he got control of himself and snapped out of it.

When Leroy had turned back into himself, you traveled to Gatherhold for some drinks and halfling songs in the Green Dragon tavern, then left for Kitt's tribe with Leroy and Ziolda very drunk. Two of Kitt's childhood bullies mocked you all before you just pressed on for Weyla's. Kitt asked Weyla about the mark on her neck and about the Ghostwise halfling name that Weyla made up.

After Weyla forced some medicine into Leroy's mouth, thinking he looked sick, she said she made up the name because she didn't like people calling Kitt a freak and wanted Kitt to believe that her telepathy was normal and cool. She also remembered that Kitt's mother had a mark sort of similar to the mark Kitt has, though it was a different shape and on her palm. She didn't know if Kitt's father had one as well, but you theorized that he did, and that Kitt's parents had two separate marks: the Mark of Healing and the Mark of Hospitality. Knowing that the mark appeared when Kitt picked up the dragon bone, you all went with Weyla to the remains of the dragon's lair.

Weyla wild shaped into a monkey to go through the lair and climb down what used to be a seaside cliff with a tunnel leading to part of the dragon's horde. Then she didn't want to turn back into an old woman again and just ran around as a monkey. You found some gold and silver and a drinking horn made from one of the spikes of a topaz dragon's back. The horn changed from yellow to purple at Kitt's touch, but no one else's, and Kitt learned that it can produce a variety of mundane liquids as well as a potion of healing or a potion of climbing once per day.

Then you send Hulrik a message to come pick you up on his way to Sharn. He flew you over the Mournlands and dropped you off in New Cyre, where you chatted with Flynt, asking him a lot about the Order of the Emerald Claw, topaz dragons, and dragonmarks. Then you helped him out a bit around the settlement and messed around with Ferno's ability to teleport people.
