Arc 3 — The Dark Pack

Sessions 11–16

Session 11 Session 12 Session 13 Session 14 Interlude 3 Session 15 Session 16

Session 11

Last time, while Ziolda and Leroy were messing around with Ferno, Flynt asked Kitt what kind of crazy stuff the party is involved in, as you were all clearly hiding things from him. Kitt decided to tell him everything that's happened to you since you met in the Nightwood, including the partial dragonmark on her neck.

Flynt asked if he could examine the mark. When he cast a higher level detect magic spell on it, he got a sense of a strange mixture of necromancy and evocation. Sort of hurting and healing. When he cast the spell, the mark also grew, with a pattern starting to form on the right side, asymmetrical to the left side.

After blowing Flynt's mind with several more details you forgot to mention of the insane things you've done, you departed for Glyphstone Keep, where you learned that a Silver Moon ranger named Andwyn had recently killed a werewolf and barely survived to make it back to the keep. Captain Petrus Stonerun was planning an expedition to learn more about the werewolf in the Graywall Mountains that make up the border between Breland and Droaam.

You joined the rangers and made for the northern end of the mountains, beneath the Six Kings monument. But you found a Droaamite army camped there and decided it would be too dangerous for all of you to try to sneak through the camp. Instead you took the more difficult path up to the monument, climbing up the steep cliffs.

After a couple sketchy climbs, aided by a climbing potion from Kitt's horn, the rope of climbing, and Kitt wildshaping into a big spider, you were attacked by a liondrake. While you fought the liondrake, a fleecemane lion snuck up on you from the other side and killed one of the rangers before pouncing on Leroy and nearly killing him too. When both creatures were dead, you needed to make one more climb and let Megan roll for it and she rolled a nat 1 on the luck check.

Nevertheless, you made it to the top and searched the werewolf's body, which Andwyn had hidden. You found a symbol for the Dark Pack and Leroy realized that the werewolves must be building the Dark Pack back up to infiltrate armies and destroy them from within to make conquering new lands easier.

You all hid when you heard the Droaamite army marching up the path that runs along the Graywall Mountains on the Droaam side. It appeared that they were heading for Graywall Outpost, an old fortress from the Last War that's currently being held by House Deneith, though with little guard. Petrus said that this needed to be taken care of, but you all needed to get to the Great Crag to meet Moridar, the undercover Silver Moon operative. As you were about to set out for the town of Graywall, Petrus offered to have Andwyn go with you and you immediately became paranoid.

It turned out your paranoia was justified, because when you put wolfsbane oil on Andwyn's hand, his skin started to react violently, and you realized that he must have hidden the fact that he was bitten in the fight. Leroy managed to keep his bloodlust under control, and you conceded to Petrus's wish to cure Andwyn rather than kill him. But the only way that you know could work is to have someone cast a Remove Curse spell on him.

You quickly did a lot of texting with your allies:

Kitt with Flynt, who is friendly with the king of Breland and could try to find a cleric in his court. Ziolda with Queen Diani about sending someone from the Silver Flame without drawing the attention of the Council of Cardinals. And Leroy with Hulrik to say "Wazzup" and ask if he can act as a getaway driver if you run into trouble in Droaam

In the end, it was decided that Andwyn would be taken to King Boranel's estate in Arakhain, where he would be guarded by Brelish soldiers until a healer could be found by Diani or Flynt.

Then you went to Graywall, where you were drawn into the Blood Pits to win a token of Sora Maenya's favor that will allow you access into the court at the Great Crag. You handily beat four rounds of monsters, but things got a little dicey when you were faced with a Mage Hunter that singled Kitt out and nearly killed her.

In the end, Kitt was able to land the finishing blow and you each received a medallion you can present to get into the Court as well as your earnings from the bets you placed on yourselves. You took a rest before heading out of town for the Great Crag.

Session 12

Last time, you left the Blood Pits of Graywall and headed to the Great Crag with the medallions that would grant you access to the court. Once you arrived in the Great Crag, you found a shop called Unkel and Auntiques: Magic from a Lost Realm. The owner, a hobgoblin named Unkel, tried to trick you into buying his stock for way more than it was worth by making up stories about the items' pasts and what magic they could do.

Once you figured out that he was lying, you bullied him into selling you most of his stock for most of your Blood Pit winnings. You purchased a lantern with a black candle that burns green but doesn't consume the candle, a pearl necklace that supposedly turns red at the full moon, an ampoule of bright green potion (the properties of which Kitt is still trying to figure out), a silver pocketwatch that can be used twice a day to let the target immediately expend up to half of their hit dice to heal, a chest with a strange hexagonal locking mechanism on top, a letter that is magically sealed, and a journal that's heavily redacted.

Then you went to the Wicked Lion tavern. Kitt made friends with the goblin owner, Grogina, while Leroy went to talk to a gross gnoll whom people call Ortok the Mangy. Ziolda went over to a goliath monk who told her to shoot him. He caught the crossbow bolt and then asked her to swing her glaive at him, which cut off part of his hand. Meanwhile, Peanut beat a minotaur in a wrestling match.

When you left the tavern to head up to the court, Ziolda caught sight of a man she recognized by sight, but not by name, as a deserter from the Thrane army. He went down an alleyway and appeared to buy some drugs from a tiny man. When the gnome came toward you, you all made a big scene of bumping into him to find out that under his hood he was a deep gnome. He hurried away after making sure he didn’t drop any of his drugs and you continued to the court.

In the court, you met several people including:

The Daughters of Sora Kell: Sora Katra, the leader of the country, Sora Maenya, the one in charge of the army, and Sora Teraza, the oracle, with her pet basilisk Suja

Moridar, the undercover half-orc Silver Moon agent

Motra, a vedalken who supplies the Blood Pits with creatures

Skrunch, a stone golem to whom Kitt gave a magic stone, which he ate

Rowan, the tiefling seneschal of the court

And the bodyguards of Sora Maenya, a half-troll named Dug and an ogre named Dahg

Kitt, Ziolda, and Peanut provided a distraction in the form of getting Peanut to wrestle people so Leroy could sneak away and talk with Moridar without anyone overhearing. Peanut easily beat Dug and Dahg and then had a close match against Skrunch, beating the stone golem in an upset that still doesn't fully make sense.

Leroy and Moridar discussed information Moridar had gathered on the members of the Court, who he trusts and who he is suspicious of. Among those absent from the court were Moridar's friend Ilze and a goliath named Kira. Ilze had told Moridar that Kira sometimes disappears during the night while the pair are out on scouting missions for Sora Maenya. This made Moridar suspicious of Kira, but he maintained his cover and didn't act on his suspicions.

When somebody approached Leory and Moridar, Moridar pulled Leroy through a door into what turned out to be Sora Teraza's study, with tarot cards, a frame on the wall with a blank parchment and an inscription that read "Map" in elvish, a glass dome containing nesting mimics, a metal rod with small ivory scales, and a long mirror.

Leroy and Moridar looked into the mirror. Moridar saw werewolves running through a swamp and put together that this was probably the Shadow Marches. Leroy saw Flynt and Jaela attempting to cure Andwyn of lycanthropy. But when Jaela started to cast her spell, Andwyn shifted into werewolf form and lunged at Jaela. Flynt pushed her aside and was bitten on the arm instead. Then the vision faded.

You all met back up and left the Court to get a room at Grogina's tavern. While you were investigating your purchases and discussing next moves, Moridar spoke from the window. He was so stealthy, it seemed like he had just passed through the glass somehow. With him, you made plans to get a ride from Hulrik to the Shadow Marches.

While you waited for Hulrik, you contacted Jaela through Queen Diani and King Boranel. You found out that Jaela had healed Flynt's bite wound and Andwyn had been cured, but Flynt had vanished without a word to anyone. You got no response from him when you tried to send him messages.

When you got to the Shadow Marches, Avrok "Skullcleaver" d'Tharashk, an orc barbarian with the Mark of Finding, led you through the swamps until you came to some ancient ruins where a werewolf with a streak of pink hair, matching Kira's description, was meeting with four other werewolves and a human in plate armor.

You snuck up and attacked, with Leroy, Ziolda, Moridar, and Avrok up in the mix and Kitt zapping enemies with Call Lightning from the sidelines. You defeated the werewolves, killing all of them except for Kira.

Session 13

Last time, you interrogated Kira using Zone of Truth and a rope doused in wolfsbane oil. She told you that the Dark Pack intends to make Graywall Outpost their new den. Ilze, who was to be given command of the outpost is also a werewolf, a fact that made Moridar furious, as he considered her a friend. Kira also told you that the Dark Pack is being led by someone known as The Umbral Shade.

You brought Kira back to The Great Crag so the Daughters of Sora Kell could ask her questions and know that you spoke the truth about the werewolf traitors. When you reached The Great Crag, Kitt was accosted by the deserter Ziolda had recognized. He was clearly high, with the whites of his eyes tinted blue and his irises almost nonexistent around enormous pupils. He was screaming at Kitt that he needed more azurium. When Leroy tried to pull him off her, he blasted Leroy with a 3rd level magic missile.

Dug and Dahg hurried down to arrest the man and Dug took him to the dungeons under the Court. The Daughters were notified of your presence, and they all came to the great hall to question Kira in another Zone of Truth. When Kira wouldn't answer, Sora Katra shattered her legs. When Kira had explained everything, Sora Teraza's basilisk turned her to stone. Sora Katra sent Dahg and Skrunch after Dug, who had been revealed as a traitor, and informed you that taking out the werewolves was not something they could do, as their power is localized to The Great Crag. And should the werewolves succeed, the three of them, even at their strongest, could not contend with an entire army. They asked you to take care of this threat for them.

Before you left, Sora Teraza invited you to her study to give you her rod inlaid with ivory scales as a token of thanks for unmasking the werewolves. When Ziolda touched the rod, she felt a slight tingle in her fingers. Sora Teraza also let you look into her mirror, which she said shows images of the present as they relate to achieving one's deepest goals. Ziolda, wanting to find the answer to what she is, saw an island with a giant diamond through the center reflecting golden light. Kitt, wanting to find Flynt, and Leroy, wanting to find a way to rid the world of werewolves, both saw Flynt beaten and unconscious in a cage.

As you set out on Hulrik's airship, Ziolda sent Queen Diani an update on the situation with the werewolves and Kitt summoned some ravens to carry messages from Leroy to your Silver Moon and Crimson Knights contacts. In addition to the ravens, a falcon began circling overhead and Kitt recognized that it was Ferno. Ferno attempted to mime that Flynt was captured by large, winged creatures and taken north. You debated splitting up to save him and go to the outpost, and in the end, you sent Moridar ahead to the outpost on foot while you flew north to the Byeshk Mountains.

As you snuck through the tunnels there, you saw several monstrous dragonlike creatures. At the very back of the tunnel system, in what looked like an old mine, you found Flynt in a cage near two corrupted dragon eggs. Ziolda broke Flynt free and healed him a bit, but he was still extremely exhausted. He told you that after Andwyn had bitten him, Andwyn had been cured. And that night, he had a vision of Kitt being attacked by werewolves. His speaking stone had fallen and shattered when Andwyn attacked him, so he left to come find you and make sure you were all okay. But he was attacked by these creatures and sent Ferno to find you instead.

You destroyed the corrupted eggs and, much to Flynt's embarrassment, Ziolda carried him in a bjorn like a small child. But you encountered a few dragon monsters searching for the source of your wet footprints and you had to fight to make a quick escape. Flynt and Peanut both nearly died, but you made it out and traveled toward Graywall Outpost. Ziolda and Leroy were jerks to Flynt for just trying to be brave and a hero, and Kitt merely said she was glad he was okay before abruptly leaving the room.

At the outpost, you saw your Silver Moon and Crimson Knights allies gathered. The outpost had already been taken by the Droaam army that was camped on the plateau before it with their siege weapons. To Leroy's surprise, among the Knights was their leader, Orlan, who had come with Bervodd after heard the situation.

Knowing the full moon would reveal who among the Droaamites were werewolves, the group decided to wait until just before the full moon to announce Sora Maenya's declaration that all members of the Dark Pack are traitors and anyone who does not surrender will be executed on the spot.

While your allies began to cause a distraction by storming the outpost and the remaining werewolves defending their new den, you, along with Moridar, snuck over the wall to take out the werewolf leadership yourselves. As you snuck through a sort of great hall, you heard a chittering, clicking noise, followed by a whoosh overhead. Then a massive crow with five red eyes and mandibles leapt from the shadows.

Session 14

Last time, you started by fighting a deep crow alongside your friend Moridar as you snuck into Graywall Outpost to kill the leader of the Dark Pack. During the fight, Leroy struggled against the full moon as he saw visions of an elf child named Valdren who grew up to be the first werewolf. Wanting to take control of all of Khorvaire, he made a pact with a powerfully magical being to gain this ability.

After you defeated the deep crow, you made your way through the fortress as the battle outside picked up. A rock launched by a catapult smashed the hall you were in, but you managed to keep going and make it to the heart of the fortress. While Leroy listened at the large, barred doors in the entrance hall to hear a woman chanting something indistinct on the other side, Ziolda found a secret side passage that led you around to the other side of the doors.

Leroy had more visions of Ilze growing up and training with her orcish father before being attacked and cursed with lycanthropy. Over months, she started to lose herself. First blacking out at the full moon and killing a halfling shepherd and his flock, then desperately tying herself to a tree and attacking herself instead, and finally snapping and killing her family.

When you emerged from the secret passage, you heard Ilze summoning the being known as The Umbral Shade, whom you deduced was Valdren's spirit. Thinking the lone werewolf below was Ilze, Leroy and Ziolda leapt down to attack. But the werewolf was actually Grannith, the monk whose fingers Ziolda had accidentally chopped off with her silvered glaive. Ilze finished summoning Valdren, who took Leroy's twinblade and expertly used it to attack Leroy, taunting him about not having earned it.

A second werewolf appeared, and you took this one to be Ilze as well. Kitt summoned Hugo, the giant constrictor snake, to restrain the werewolf, and only after he started screaming at the sight of Peanut did you realize it was actually Dug. Moridar used moonbeam to temporarily revert the werewolves to their normal forms so they couldn't bite any of you, and he darted around, attacking and vanishing into the shadows. Meanwhile, Ilze was doing the same. She tried to climb up and go after Kitt, but Leroy teleported up and powerbombed her back to the ground, saving Kitt and allowing Moridar to hit her with the moonbeam.

After you took care of Grannith and Peanut fittingly took out Dug, Valdren went after Kitt. Runes lit up the room and started attacking you like whips and causing debris to fall on you. The battle started taking a bad turn as you ran out of potions and Valdren put down Leroy before counterspelling Ziolda's last healing spell. Ilze was out of the moonbeam long enough to shift back into a werewolf and bit Kitt, cursing her to become a werewolf. Then Peanut saved Kitt by killing Ilze. But Valdren worked his way through the rest of the party until everyone, including Peanut and Moridar, were dead.

Kitt found herself looking over the battlefield outside, with every person lit up purple. Ziolda found herself with Tira Miron near a huge silver flame. Tira told Ziolda that she came to this place after she died defeating the Great Demon, and that she doesn't fully understand what's going on. Ziolda saw that Tira had the same ice blue and silver-ringed eyes as her and stepped into the flame to discover that she is bound to Tira in some way, through a sort of spectral, golden strand. There were also two other strands from which Ziolda got a sense of death, and a fourth that had been somehow severed.

Leroy found himself drifting downward toward the spirit realm and thought about how Kitt and Ziolda needed him, and if they died, it was because of their choice to help with his personal vendetta. A voice reached out to Leroy and offered him a chance to try again and to save his friends, but in return, he would offer himself in this being's service. Leroy agreed, and he received a ring to give him the metaphorical power and presence of a dragon.

As he made this deal, Kitt found she could move again and she felt pulled toward the battlefield, where one person was different than the others, having a glowing golden heart at the center of the purple veins and arteries. She figured out that this was Flynt and told him that the party had died and reassured him that he could make it to help them. Flynt turned into a cheetah and sprinted for the fortress with Ferno teleporting him to keep him from being attacked.

Leroy woke up as Skrunch busted down the doors and let Flynt in to revivify Kitt with her sending stone. As Leroy got up to fight Valdren again, he took back his twinblade and Flynt went to revivify Ziolda and Peanut, burning the last of your party's sending stones. Leroy defeated Valdren and finally earned his twinblade.

Skrunch gave everyone who needed one a ride back to the Silver Moon and Crimson Knight camp while Leroy carried Moridar's body and insistently tried to get Kitt to bite Flynt to maybe remove her curse. You met up with your allies and took stock of how many had died in the fighting and then tried to get some rest.

Interlude 3

Session 15

Last time, Leroy and Flynt tried to figure out how to cure Kitt of lycanthropy. Flynt also voiced some of his feelings about Leroy's philosophies after speaking with Peanut and learning about the party's history with werewolves. Leroy and Flynt brought Orlan in for help, since he's an expert on blood magic. You decided that you needed a combination of Flynt's blood, whatever spell he had reflexively cast on Andwyn, and the use of a Siberys dragonshard as the material component.

Meanwhile, Ziolda met with Tira again in her pseudo-dreams. Tira expressed her regret that her legacy is as a warrior and a killer, not as a protector, and advised Ziolda that there is just as much honor in protecting people as there is in slaying evil creatures. She gave her shield to Ziolda and it became Ziolda's Aura of Protection.

Kitt had a dream about running from a pack of werebears led by Leroy and Bervodd. They cornered her, but a figure that turned out to be Ilze jumped in and kept Leroy back before taking Kitt off the cliff to safety. Ilze apologized for spreading her curse to Kitt and offered what little she could give in death to aid Kitt. Ilze's spirit joined Kitt's Spirit Shroud and allows Kitt to increase her AC by 2 when concentrating on the spell. Kitt's dragonmark has also grown larger since coming back from the dead.

Leroy woke up from a nap out in the middle of the camp to a tiny crystal pseudodragon rooting through his pack. The pseudodragon, whom Leroy named Oswald, took Leroy's Hellish Rebuke scroll. It interested him because it's the same kind of magic as him. You questioned him for a while, but he clearly knows nothing except that he has a natural sense for magical things.

The party made a quick stop in The Great Crag to get a reward from the Daughters of Sora Kell. In addition to owing you a favor, Sora Katra gave you a wand that allows you to cast any 1st level wizard spell once per day, with a total of ten uses. You set up a meeting for signing a treaty to declare Droaam a sovereign nation. Then, you looked in Sora Teraza's mirror again. Ziolda saw the same vision. Flynt and Leroy, wanting to cure Kitt, saw Andwyn's Silver Moon tattoo. And Kitt, wanting to understand her dragonmark, saw the image of Cardinal Halidor.

Flynt asked Ziolda to train him to fight and taught Kitt the Revivify spell. He also gave her a piece of obsidian glass whittled into the shape of a claw with druidic runes to give her the Produce Flame cantrip.

You made another quick stop in Flamekeep to get five more Siberys dragonshards from the queen to use as sending stones or as Revivify components. Ziolda also found that the scar over Cardinal Halidor's heart from when Lord Soth killed him is similar to Kitt's dragonmark.

At Arcanix, Kitt took a few classes and heard of a former student named Alkes Rigel, who had been very interested in using astromancy to find a mythical island called Draksholm. This aligned with Ziolda's research which found that a book called Draksholm: Uncovering Myth through Astromantic Study had been checked out years ago by Alkes and never returned.

Leroy had to deal with Oswald trying to fight a wizard's raven familiar and appeased him by giving him the arcane stopwatch. He couldn't find anything referencing "The Great Dragon" but he did research sapphire dragons.

Flynt tracked down a recipe for something called a Chaos Potion in the library and gave it to Kitt.

Ziolda researched dragonmarks and found illustrations of many different marks, but none that matched Kitt's mark. But she also found that some information on the subject may have been removed from the library.

After a while, you attempted to cure Kitt's lycanthropy by having her bite Flynt's arm while he tried to use a dragonshard to cast Remove Curse. The first attempt failed, though Flynt succeeded in casting the spell. Then you realized that the vision about Andwyn's Silver Moon tattoo meant you needed to have some silver to weaken Kitt's werewolf resilience. Leroy gave Kitt a silver tattoo on her arm in the shape of Peanut, and the second attempt to remove the curse succeeded.

When the curse was removed, Kitt and Flynt had a shared vision of the draconians corrupting and hatching a dragon egg that produced three even larger and stronger looking draconians.

Session 16

Last time, we started off with Leroy chasing Oswald into the Quandrix campus to the lab of an artificer named Edvard Covenridge. After Leroy got Oswald under control, Edvard showed Leroy his work on what he calls "The Wonderful Mechanical Man", which is his attempt to reproduce some of House Cannith's work on the warforged.

Oswald was very interested in the Khyber dragonshards Edvard was trying to use to power the WMM and later revealed that he stole one, along with at least one stick of dynamite. He asked that if Leroy comes across any information about warforged, that Leroy will aid him in fixing whatever's wrong with the WMM.

Meanwhile Ziolda came across the name Traelor in very old records of a request for information on creating powerful arcane barriers. She felt a pang in her chest when she read the name, similar to how she feels connected to Tira but more painful. Investigation by the party led to the conclusion that this paper is about as old as the name Demon Wastes. Leroy had heard of a magical barrier put in place there called the Labyrinth, which keeps the horrors of that land contained.

Kitt received an invitation from her necromancy professor to join the Mages of High Sorcery in the Order of the Black Robes. She learned that members of House Vol were historically very gifted necromancers, but that no members of the Blood of Vol were permitted in the Order of the Black Robes. Information about the dragonmark of House Vol had been redacted from the library, but Professor Zalyr told Kitt that they had the Mark of Death. The mark was strange among the dragonmarks in that it could be found across many races. Though the founders of the house were elves, all who bore the mark were welcome in their ranks.

Leroy chased Oswald again into a room under the library labeled as "The Snarl". He found Oswald getting ready to throw dynamite into the magical anomaly there and stopped him. Oswald promised not to use the dynamite if Leroy touched the anomaly. Leroy went into a trance, finding himself in a cavern with a strange magical creature, which he defeated. Then a figure showed up and told him to leave. The Great Dragon spoke through Oswald telling Leroy to get rid of this figure. Leroy bit the figure's neck as a werebear and came out of the trance to find a library worker with a nametag that said, "Brian" dead on the floor. He and Oswald burned the body and Oswald scooped up some of the ashes to keep. The Great Dragon said his method of obedience was informative and gave him a boon, the Gift of the Gem Dragon.

Ziolda felt herself pulled by her pendant down into the excavations on the Lorehold campus where she found an excavation into a sort of temple to the pagan god Dol Arrah. Inside were three doors. Ziolda felt drawn to the door with the red and silver rose on it and descended into a crypt, where she found a coffin labeled Khardam Silvereye, Dol Arrah's favored champion, Solamnia's greatest hero. She defeated the ghostly figure of a Knight of the Rose, bringing his spirit peace. Then she had a vision of Khardam leading a company of knights into battle against an army of undead and a skeletal dragon. Coming out of the vision, she felt a connection to these knights and gained the Squire of Solamnia feat.

Kitt went through the trial to join the Order of the Black robes, navigating an invisible maze easily with faerie fire and defeating the shambling mound at the center. She was given an ebony wand and black robes, which she put on under her bone armor, and she gained the Initiate of High Sorcery feat. Professor Zalyr told her that she can attempt future trials later to gain new knowledge of enchantment and necromancy from the Order.

Lastly, you got a message from Tiergon, your spy among the Order of the Emerald Claw. He said, "The Order of the Emerald Claw is sending knights and mages to Q'barra. They're looking for something called the Fount of Inarieth."

Hulrik agreed to take you as far as Gatherhold, but he can't fly over the mountains to Q'barra because of the severe storms there. You decided that you would get off in Gatherhold and go see Weyla before crossing the Blade Desert toward Q'barra.
