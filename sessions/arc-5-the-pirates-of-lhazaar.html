The Chronicle · Arc 5

Arc 5 — The Pirates of Lhazaar

Sessions 22–25

Session 22 Session 23 Session 24 Interlude 5 Session 25

Session 22

Last time, you arrived in the Whistler's Whirlpool tavern and Ziolda drunkenly went up to a man you suspected was Alkes and tried to get him to show her what he was writing in his notebook. After he got up and walked away, Kitt and Leroy had better luck talking to him while Izzy stood up on the balcony looking out for anything out of the ordinary.

Alkes told you about his research into the mythical island known as Draksholm and how he had been falsely imprisoned so some organization could try to get their hands on his star map. He showed you the book he had checked out of the Arcanix library long ago and opened it to a page with an artist's rendering of Draksholm based on descriptions in legend. It appeared very similar to the island Ziolda had seen in Sora Teraza's mirror.

Izzy told you that the parrot that had been on a pirate's shoulder had left. A while later, it came back, at which point Kitt and Izzy swapped places and Kitt spoke to the parrot telepathically with Speak with Animals. She crit to convince the parrot that she was the parrot god and learned that there were many other pirates waiting outside. The parrot had been a signal for them to gather outside the tavern.

Then the whirlpool erupted like a geyser and both Leroy and the pirate went for Alkes. In the confusion, Alkes lost control of the Wristpocket spell that was hiding his star map and it was snatched up by the pirate. You told Izzy and Alkes to get somewhere safe and avoid the fighting. Alkes called up to someone named Kass on the balcony, and the woman with the veil jumped out of a window to chase after the pirate.

You all went to chase down the pirate as well and a huge fight ensued during which the orb changed hands several times, Kassandra was fully killed, strange figures in black robes emerged from hiding places to also try to get the orb, Leroy yanked someone off of a bridge with his grappling crossbow, and the party found themselves outmatched against the black robed mages. After Ziolda had been knocked out and Kitt was nearly knocked out as well, Leroy and Peanut ended up with the orb. One of the mages offered a deal to Leroy that they would let you all live if you handed over the orb.

Leroy took the deal and you teamed up with the black robed people, whom Leroy noticed had the pointed ears of elves under their hoods, to take out the remaining pirates. After the fighting was done, the elves scattered, taking the orb with them. You saw that the one elf that had been killed was a pile of ash, presumably from some sort of self-immolation to hide any clues to his identity.

Leroy sent Oswald to start looting bodies while you revivified Kassandra, and you later found out that Oswald acquired a great deal of gunpowder from the pirates. He may be making pipe bombs to replace the dynamite he had run out of.

You returned to your room at the Gold Dragon Inn and spoke some more with Kassandra and Alkes. They had both been falsely imprisoned, with Kassandra being charged with espionage after simply examining a merchant ship traveling to Lake Galifar. She noticed that it was from the Lhazaar Principalities, so she was suspicious of pirates. When she examined it, she found no goods or cargo to be sold. She was captured before she could learn more.

The pair escaped from Dreadhold Prison along with the others who were posted on the notice board: Shadow, Libella, Dozier, and Whit. But the others had focused less on escaping and more on killing as many people in the prison as they could. Alkes had used invisibility spells to get himself and Kassandra out and leave the others behind.

The murder had especially angered Kassandra, who follows The Path of the Light. You noticed the sun-shaped pendant around her neck, marking her monastic order and remembered seeing a similar one in Droaam. You then put together that she must be the Sun mentioned in the poem, and Alkes is the Stars.

You went to sleep for the night. Kitt was awoken by someone putting a knife to her neck and immediately cast Thunderwave on her attacker. Unfortunately, knocking him back meant the knife dragged across her throat and she was hit by a very strong poison.

The attackers turned out to be Shadow and Libella. Shadow said you had three days to find the orb and bring it to him or Kitt would die. Only he knows the way to cure her, and if you kill him, that knowledge dies with him. He and Libella teleported away.

Session 23

Last time, you went on a mission to retrieve Alkes's star map so Shadow would cure Kitt. Using Locate Object, you tracked it down briefly in the compound of House Thuranni before it vanished. Kitt turned into a cat to slip through the bars of the fence and figured out that the fences around the compound had an illusion that hid the presence of various guards and house members within.

Then you decided that Kitt and Oswald would set fire to one of the buildings to create a distraction while the rest of you, with help from Alkes's Invisibility spells, would hop the fence and sneak into the building where you last sensed the star map. It went well until Peanut was spotted and had to jump back over the fence and run away, pursued by guards, to find Izzy.

When you all got into the building, which turned out to belong to Elar d'Thuranni, the head of the house, you began searching for where the orb might have gone. While searching, Kassandra found information that suggested that House Thuranni had worked with Lord Soth to help him assassinate Cardinal Halidor. Elar d'Thuranni has devices called asters that can have a demiplanar gate attached to them. The aster was disguised as an inkwell and smuggled into the citadel in Flamekeep, where Lord Soth came out of his demiplane to kill Cardinal Halidor.

You also found records that House Thuranni runs the Boons of the Briny shop as a front for smuggling operations and they've repeatedly hired someone named Conroy Thrice, who captains the Aeon's Raptor.

You found one of these asters on the underside of a bookshelf and activated it to turn the bookcase into a portal to Elar's private lab. Leroy was compelled to drink a potion that turned out to be something called a doppelixir that tried to suck his blood. Once you got it off him, Leroy picked up 8 darts with coastline reaper venom and Ziolda picked up a vial of purple fungus spores. Ziolda also found a strange deep violet cat with a colorless crystal embedded into its forehead and she decided you would free it after taking care of Elar.

You went downstairs and found Elar trying to open the star map. Leroy immediately tackled him and Ziolda tried to cut his head off but was stopped by his animated cloak. Then two sea elf corpses came to life and tried to smother Kitt and Alkes. Elar got free and cast a Cloudkill spell that nearly killed everyone, but when things started to look really bad, Leroy managed to break Elar's concentration and end the spell. Alkes knocked Elar out and Ziolda killed him.

On the way out, Ziolda loosed the rest of the doppelixirs to attack anyone who enters the lab to investigate. She also let the cat free and it followed you out of the demiplane before disappearing into the city.

You escaped from the scene largely unnoticed, except for Ziolda, who was flying over the buildings with glowing wings, and Kassandra, who fired bursts of sunlight from the roof of the building to distract guards and help Alkes sneak away.

You all met back up at the Wandering Inn and investigated the star map. Ziolda put together from the prophecy that Alkes and Kassandra, as the Stars and the Sun, needed to both use magic to illuminate the orb. Then you placed one of each type of dragonshard in the light to diffract it into constellations that will help you navigate to Draksholm.

You made plans for meeting the pirates and getting Kitt cured. Izzy constructed a fake orb, and Kassandra forged a map with dwarven script to put inside it. Kassandra and Alkes expressed that they believe the pirates need to be dealt with, or they will be a threat to innocents all over the coast. But Alkes also pointed out that the pirates would have a severe numbers advantage.

You decided that you would send Oswald into the pirates' ship under Alkes's Invisibility spell, and he would wait for your signal to blow up the ship's storage of gunpowder to even the odds a bit.

You sent some messages to your allies before getting ready to head out to meet the pirates.

Leroy sent a message to Hulrik, saying, “Have discovered something big. New lands, not charted yet. Willing to pay. Could be riches there. Have to go over large sea.”

Ziolda sent a message to Queen Diani, saying, “Detect magic on every ink bottle for portals for assassins. Don't destroy. Keep safe. Because we might be able to use it.”

And Kitt sent a message to Flynt, saying, “Not sure where you're at right now. In Regalport. Going to go on a journey. If you're nearby and want to join. Hope your research is going well. Any updates? P.S. Got cursed again (frowny face), but we're managing. Love, Kitt.”

Session 24

Last time, you had some chats with your friends before you set off to face the pirates.

Izzy talked with Leroy and Kitt about how the Sharn fairy tales almost always center around a character named Lord Soth being the villain. The stories all change, and people create their own heroes for their own versions, but Lord Soth's story stays fairly consistent. He was a knight who defied his king and the king's advisors to search for a talisman that would give him the power to end war in the land. He found it and succeeded in ending the war, but it quickly started to corrupt him.

Ziolda spoke with Kassandra, who is a kalashtar and sensitive to the spirit realm. She told Ziolda that she sensed that Ziolda was not quite right and there was something odd coming from Ziolda's pendant in addition to the divine power she uses as a paladin. Ziolda told her about being undead and about the other paladins. Being from a monastery where the kingdom of Solamnia used to be, Kassandra had access to ancient knowledge including the history of Solamnia and the Solamnic language. She offered to help Ziolda track down anything related to Khardam Silvereye should Ziolda ask.

Leroy and Alkes talked about how Leroy was torn apart and teleported when he was inside the mist during the Mourning. Leroy said that the city of Making seemed to be the epicenter of the incident, which reaffirmed Alkes's hypothesis that the artificers in Cyre had been using some new power source stronger than Khyber dragonshards and that they either pushed it too far or experienced some sort of meltdown event. He started forming this hypothesis while studying the remains of the very warforged colossus that Ziolda was fighting when she died.

After that, you went to meet with the pirates and got Kitt cured of her curse. Kitt pretended to be the parrot god again to get Shadow's parrot away from the ship before Oswald blew it up. Then you rushed back into the pirate camp and proceeded to wreck the whole crew.

After the fight, Kitt got the parrot to fly down and land on Alkes's shoulder, Leroy crit on a DC 30 check to investigate the flaming wreckage of the ship, and you set about chopping off the heads of the pirate leaders to bring back to Regalport so you could collect the bounties. You decided that Leroy, Kitt and Izzy should be the ones to collect the bounties since everyone else is either wanted or was seen outside House Thuranni's headquarters.

Interlude 5

Session 25

Last time, you went to collect the bounties on the pirates and Leroy got into an argument with the clerk at the offices of the Seadragons, the royal navy of the Lhazaar Principalities. The clerk was suspicious of the timing of accomplished killers showing up right when the leader of House Thuranni was assassinated.

Izzy was able to smooth things over when she and Kitt showed their dragonmarks and said they were members of House Ghallanda with Leroy as their hired protection. She made it clear to the clerk and to you that accusing a member of one dragonmarked house of attacking another is a serious thing, and the clerk backed down.

Kitt saw a wanted poster for Conroy Thrice, and you eventually made a deal to help get him and his first mate, Stewart, out of the city so he could fly you to Draksholm. Ziolda also rolled another nat 1 and immediately got very drunk at the bar.

On the way to Draksholm, you battled through a storm that caused the ship to malfunction before being able to relax and watch the northern lights. Ziolda and Kassandra got a feeling that spirits were involved in the lights and then some broke off and swept over the airship, confusing everyone. Everyone shook it off except for Peanut, who latched onto Ziolda for several days instead of Kitt.

Then you passed through a dense wall of clouds. In the clouds, only Kitt could see the shapes of dragonborn in boats sailing in the opposite direction. Kitt tried to get their attention, threw stuff at them, and tried casting Dispel Magic on them, but nothing had any effect. You came to the conclusion that this was likely some memory of the land being manifested in the clouds.

You eventually came to a place where the island should be, and it was just ocean. But the water had a sort of fractal, crystalline look every few seconds. Ziolda jumped in and waited for the phenomenon to occur again before diving. She emerged into daylight while the ship remained in night. Leroy joined her a moment later. And then you dove the ship through the water and out of the other side of the gate.

You landed on Draksholm and started exploring what turned out to be a library. While Alkes had Comprehend Languages active, he tried to speed through as many books as possible for you. Eventually, Kitt saw a ruby pangolin peek into the library and then leave. You followed the pangolin across the city and Kitt followed it into its burrow before digging further and emerging into a sort of arcane lab.

Once everyone else joined, you explored the lab and found that whoever was working here had been working on several projects incorporating dragonshards into objects to give them magical features. But their pet project was a chamber that utilized all three types of dragonshards to either slow down or speed up time and aging to keep an injured person in stasis or to accelerate growth of plants for new research.

Kitt explored deeper into the lab and eventually came across a room with a dull, red light. When she entered, she found the stasis chamber holding a large dragonborn with heavily dented armor that was breathing ever so slightly. The dragonshards that made up the chamber were dull, cracking, or shattered entirely, and it seemed like the magic they were sustaining was becoming weaker.
