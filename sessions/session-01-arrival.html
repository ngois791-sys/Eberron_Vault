Session Recap

Session 1 — Arrival in the City of Towers

  • Date June 1, 2026
  • Location The City of Towers

Session City of Towers

The party arrived as the sun set behind the great towers, their airship docking high above the lower wards. None of them knew the others — not yet — but a single sealed letter had drawn them all to the same smoky tavern.

What Happened

  • The characters met for the first time and (mostly) decided to trust one another.
  • A nervous broker offered them a job: recover a stolen lockbox, no questions asked.
  • A back-alley scuffle revealed they were already being followed.

Loose Threads

  • Who hired the broker, and why the secrecy?
  • The symbol on the followers' cloaks — has anyone seen it before?
