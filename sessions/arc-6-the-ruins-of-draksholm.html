Arc 6 — The Ruins of Draksholm

Sessions 26–33

Session 26

Last time, you freed Festarik, the dragonborn Lord of Draksholm, from the stasis chamber and informed him of the fate of the dragonborn.

He stepped outside and witnessed the thousands of years of ruin across the city. When he said he was no longer the Lord of Draksholm, but rather the Lord of Dust, Leroy incorrectly assumed he was having a sense of humor about it. But Festarik, or Rik as you called him, kept quiet about how that offended him.

Festarik showed you around what remained of the island. In the Hall of Whispers, he showed Leroy and Kitt the council chambers and the diamond throne in the tallest spire overlooking the city while Ziolda and Kassandra found a hidden cache of items made by Festarik’s mate, Cuprir: a dragonscale manica with Siberys dragonshards that can cast Shield on an ally, a dagger with a Khyber dragonshard that can cast Absorb Elements, and a quiver of arrows tipped with Eberron dragonshards with an unknown property that look like they could shatter in fragments. Kitt took the manica, Ziolda took the dagger, and Leroy took the arrows.

At the House of Convalescence, you each took a flask of the Golden Waters that can allow you to get maximum healing from hit dice during a short rest and regain one spell slot of up to 3rd level.

At the Gardens of Concord, Kitt found a plant that Festarik called Katharoz that was cultivated from blessed soil and can be planted to enrich the soil and clean the air of its new location. She also found a plant called Twilight Nettle that the dragonborn would use to make tea that sharpened their minds.

Then you went to the Temple of the Progenitors, where the dragonborn housed the Draconic Prophecy, the result of the greatest dragonborn mages working to see a future even the gods could not see. The prophecies--the mages' attempts to write about what they saw or experienced--were recorded on four tablets set into one of the walls of the temple. But when you looked, you saw that one had been physically removed by chipping away the stone around it, while the other three seemed to have vaporized. Closer inspection revealed that there was a fine diamond dust in the accumulated dirt around the empty spaces.

You then went to an area of the Great Diamond called the Preserve of the Ancestors, where the dragonborn would meditate, trying to sense the spirits of their ancestors and even see visions of them reflected in the faces of the diamond. But when you looked into the three faces of the diamond, you saw images of paths extending from the other sides. One leading along a luminous bridge into a dark void, one in a rocky tunnel sloping downward, and another leading through a bright jungle. Ziolda chose to enter the first one, at which point the others could no longer see the other two and had no choice but to follow Ziolda in.

Session 27

Last time, you entered the Great Diamond of Draksholm along the path of Siberys. As you passed through the diamond's surface, you saw glimpses of many things in the facets of the gem around you. Then you emerged into a dark void. A portal at the other end of a bridge opened, and a light being split you up, sending each of you through your own portals.

Ziolda found herself in the training grounds of the Grand Army of Thrane meeting her old squad. Leroy found himself briefly in the chamber of the Snarl under Arcanix before his murder victim, Brian, took him to the field where his future wife was picking berries. Kitt found herself as a child in a mansion overlooking a city. Her parents and a child version of Izzy were there as well.

Ziolda squared off against her old mentor Kel. Over and over again, Kel evaded Ziolda's attacks and struck back, stopping just short of a fatal blow and saying, "Dead." Eventually, she said, "Maybe it's just your destiny to die."

Leroy faced The Umbral Shade, the first werewolf, and Lord Soth while Brian goaded him. Brian told Leroy that he would always be a prisoner, like so many others foolish enough to make deals with powers beyond their understanding.

Kitt fought against an old mage who killed Izzy and Flynt and told Kitt that he exists to spite Eberron and to break her druids. He revealed that he sent the disease that killed Kitt's parents, believing Katija was Eberron's chosen and that the disease would kill Kitt and her father. He offered Kitt a choice. If she forsook her friends and family, never to see them again, he would not hunt them down and kill them. If she continued to fight, she would have to live the rest of her life knowing that she could have saved them.

Kitt refused and defeated the mage with the help of some summoned fastieths. Ziolda healed herself up just in time to weather five hits in a single turn from Kel, only to come back with a massive divine smite to defeat her. Leroy decided to ignore Lord Soth and to attack Brian instead, teleporting to him and running him through.

Portals opened for each of you, and you found yourselves back in the void. You briefly thought you were out of danger before a 300-foot-tall dragon made of light appeared and started attacking. You raced through portals, trying to find your way to the top level, only to find that the only thing there was the dragon's radiant breath.

The dragon began shedding scales and creating humanoid light beings that Kitt and Leroy held off as Ziolda went after the dragon. Eventually, the dragon was weakened to the point that Ziolda was able to fly in and grab its glowing heart. The heart was really a massive Siberys dragonshard inscribed with a prophecy.

Unfortunately, the light beings didn't die with the dragon, and you had to slog through them, burning a lot of spells, abilities, and hit dice. When they were all gone, you took a moment to catch your breath and translate the prophecy before choosing to enter the path of Eberron.

Session 28

Last time, you made your way through the Eberron path toward the prophecy pertaining to Kitt. You emerged from the portal to see a dragon flying overhead with the dragonshard bearing the prophecy and you started tracking the trail of its blood.

You came upon three creatures called that called themselves boughnders when Kitt used speak with animals. They said you could ride them and they would help you get to the dragon faster, as there was a wave of corruption catching up to you. When Kitt used her belt to cast the spell, she got the sense that the belt was strained, as if some magical effect tried to trigger recently but was suppressed somehow.

You rolled very well to track the dragon as you rode the boughnders and you bypassed all of the obstacles. But when you tried to hide from huge monsters below, one of them spotted you and you were forced to run as more started to close in.

The boughnders told you to go through a place called the Shadowlands to get to the tree at the heart of the jungle. You slid down a long vine into a clearing with ferns that hit you with hallucinogenic spores. After discussion, you surmised that Leroy had been seeing through the eyes of The Lord of Blades, the warforged ruler of the Forge-Sworn Empire in what used to be Cyre. Ziolda felt that she was seeing through the eyes of Traelor, the paladin whose connection to her is severed, as he created the Labyrinth on the border of the Demon Wastes. And Kitt's hallucination remains a mystery, as she saw through the eyes of someone with a dragonmark on her arm, who tried to kill herself by stabbing herself in the heart. But she failed.

You pressed on as the corruption came closer and you ran into a wall of blood-red spiderwebs. Ziolda started cutting the webs to make the tunnel through them wider, which alerted the spiders. They tried to box you into the tunnel, and you were forced to fight your way out.

When you got free and reached the tree, the roots became animated by the corruption and tried to drag you underground. Everyone was caught in them at least once, but you were able to break out using a new spell Kitt picked up from Draksholm and Leroy's stunning gauntlet.

You climbed the inside of the tree largely without incident until you reached the final bridge. The corruption withered the bridge and animated the vines. When Leroy ran across the bridge, three of the vines grabbed him and tried to strangle him while dangling him over the thousand-foot drop. Ziolda and Kitt planned to cut the vines and push Leroy back to the bridge in one move. But when Ziolda ran across the bridge, it crumbled and she was forced to jump onto Leroy's shoulders. She cut just the vine around his neck as Kitt, as the parrot god, crashed into him. You used the momentum to swing across the gap to the other side.

When you finished the climb to the top of the tree, you found the dragon's roost and Kitt took the dragonshard from it while also healing the cuts in its palm. It jumped from the tree and dropped a hundred feet before bursting into a ring of swirling mist. In the ring, you could see the dark tunnel of Khyber's path. You took a short rest to translate Kitt's prophecy and to prepare for the next challenge.

Session 29

Last time you jumped from the treetops of Eberron's domain within the Great Diamond and through a portal into Khyber's domain. Your momentum carried you out of a tunnel and flying off a ledge over a massive drop. On a huge pillar, you saw claw marks that to Kitt and Leroy appeared as gouges in the stone, but to Ziolda appeared to be filled with golden metal. You each managed to activate a mechanism that changed gravity and kept you from falling by touching the claw marks. Each of you got on a different side of a pillar and walked down through tunnels and bridges until you reached the bottom of the pit safely.

You then came to a tunnel that got narrower and narrower until you were forced to crawl. All the while you caught sight of shadows moving in your peripherals and heard the sound of slithering and the clatter of falling rocks. Kitt turned into a giant spider, which freaked out Leroy even more as the tunnel shook like it was going to collapse. Leroy and Ziolda were compelled to flee and started backing up only to be spider-hugged by Kitt and held in place while the tunnel collapsed on both sides. But when Leroy reached out to the rock ahead of you, he found that the whole thing was an illusion that dispelled as soon as he touched it.

Beyond the illusory tunnel, you walked across a frozen, mirrorlike lake, beneath which you caught a glimpse of something large and dark moving. Hurrying past this, you entered a tunnel where the mirrors climbed onto the walls as well and you found some of your reflections were missing. When the person whose reflection was missing touched the wall, they passed through into another hall with mirror images of the others. You solved some puzzles that required manipulating objects in your own hallway to affect the others. In solving one of the puzzles, you came across a lavender gem that pulsed with light and felt warm only when Kitt was holding it.

When you made it through the halls, you reunited in a chamber that had several riddles. You solved most of the riddles on the first try, giving each of the walls what the riddle specified. But when you answered wrongly, the room sapped some of your power and ability, leaving you at a disadvantage for the next room where you had to find the real dragonshard bearing the Draconic Prophecy from among several fakes before Leroy and Kitt would suffocate in the vacuum created by the dark void that had been following you. Ziolda used detect magic to determine that the dragonshards bore abjuration or illusion magic. You activated a lot of Glyph of Warding spells going after the abjuration ones but eventually found the right illusion one.

The room refilled with air and you were able to recover your lost power, and one expended ability, and take a rest while Leroy deciphered his prophecy. The prophecy described one who would be thrice cursed and chosen by Khyber as her champion, but who would turn on her and help defeat her. You surmised that Leroy's werebear curse and the effects of the mists of Cyre accounted for two of these curses, leaving one other that you predicted is related to his warlock pact but as yet unknown.

Then you pressed onward through the next portal. You emerged in the heart of the Great Diamond where you started climbing the slope of a large hill. But the hill started to move, and you jumped back as a gargantuan diamond dragon raised its head and turned to look at you with silver-ringed eyes.

Session 30

Last time, you met the diamond dragon Alderdia the first dragon and the closest thing to the gods in the world. Alderdia explained how when Khyber turned on Siberys and Eberron, it was Alderdia who kept Siberys from dying while Eberron imprisoned Khyber. Eberron has since fallen into a sort of coma with all her waning power dedicated to containing Khyber. Khyber, meanwhile, has been giving her power to warlocks throughout history to spitefully act against Eberron and Siberys from within her prison.

As a conduit for Siberys, Alderdia is responsible for Ziolda's connection to both Siberys's magic and the spirits of his former paladins. You also learned that Ziolda's state between life and death is the result of an imperfect resurrection in which her friend Alianne gave the remainder of her power and her life to bring Ziolda's spirit back to her. But this resulted in Ziolda's spirit being contained in Alianne's Silver Flame pendant, which Ziolda now wears, and the spirit possessing Ziolda's body.

Alderdia told of the draconian uprising on Draksholm that resulted in him sealing away the island in a demiplane so the dragonborn could escape and live on while the draconians were left to die on the island. As part of the magic he worked, Alderdia can no longer assume a dragonborn form or leave the Great Diamond.

As this is the first time in history that a paladin of Siberys, a druid of Eberron, and a warlock of Khyber have all allied together, Alderdia proposed a very risky mission. He wants to kill Khyber once and for all, but that can only be done by releasing her from Eberron's prison. He told you that it is essential that Khyber does not know of Eberron's weakened state, or she could break out before the party is strong enough to defeat her. To gain that strength, you need to gather the power of the gods' other champions.

Ziolda needs to complete her connections to the ancient paladins, which entails making physical connections with both Khardam and Traelor through possessions or places, as she did with Tira and Inarieth.

Kitt needs to gain the knowledge of the world's archdruids and the unique ways in which they wielded Eberron's power. One of these is Oalian, who dwells in the Eldeen Reaches. Another was Eladan Ardosar, who discovered the Snarl, founded Arcanix, and started the Order of the Black Robes.

And Leroy needs to convince Khyber that he is worthy of more power and becoming her chief warlock by defeating and removing her magic from the existing warlocks such as Lord Soth and The Great Demon.

Alderdia then projected images of your visions from within the Great Diamond onto the wall and you discussed what the images might mean in light of what you just learned.

Lastly, a storm started to pick up outside and Alderdia sensed Khyber's magic at work. He told you that since he cannot leave the Great Diamond, he would push back on the magic from inside while you go deal with whatever the cause is outside.

You exited the Great Diamond to see Festarik levitating a hundred feet above a town square, commanding the maelstrom that is tearing buildings apart. On the ground, Alkes, Kassandra, and Izzy turned around to reveal the same glowing, blue eyes as Festarik.

Session 31

Last time, you fought The Lord of Dust and your possessed friends, who took on draconic features through Khyber's magic. Izzy had a shrieking breath weapon, Kassandra had spectral wings, and Alkes was covered in spectral dragon scales.

During the fight, Leroy and Kitt were being petrified and Leroy reached out to Khyber, saying that he knows about what Siberys and Eberron did and that he wants to help her. He asked for more power, and Khyber broke him free of the petrification.

With some help from Alderdia, you managed to defeat the warlock and free your friends, though they were all badly hurt by the effects of the magic. Festarik, now lucid, remembered all the things he had done under Khyber's influence. He didn't knowingly make a deal with Khyber, but his drive to understand magic gave her a measure of control over him.

Festarik, while possessed, made the draconians that attacked Draksholm. Cuprir knocked him out and put him in stasis in the hopes that she would be able to heal him and fix his mind after things were settled. But then the dragonborn were forced to leave. Among them was at least one dragonborn who knew how to make draconians and the knowledge has survived until today.

Festarik asked you to remove Khyber's magic from him so he couldn't be controlled to do any terrible things anymore. You all buffed Leroy as best you could, but when Leroy failed to remove the magic, Festarik asked you to kill him instead. Leroy obliged and Festarik’s body crumbled to dust.

Interlude 6

Session 32

Last time, you left Draksholm and flew to the Mror Holds to meet up with Hulrik. On the way, you discovered that the strange cat from the Thuranni lab had stowed away on the ship and was playing with Ziolda's pendant at night. Ziolda named the cat Zelda.

In the Mror Holds, you met Hulrik's family and took the diamond you found in the Endworld Mountains to a wizard. The wizard said the diamond was a Blod Diamond and performed a ritual with Leroy's blood, as Leroy had seen in one of the visions of the Great Diamond. The diamond will vibrate when close to a blood relative of the one whose blood is in the diamond. When the ritual was performed, Leroy briefly saw the blood form the silhouette of an elven man, whom Leroy surmised was his son.

After meeting with the wizard, you went to look at Mirror Lake and the mountains that make up the valley there. While the Ironroot Mountains to the west are relatively free of snow, the Hoarfrost Mountains to the east are perpetually covered with snow, despite the mountain ranges being very similar. Hulrik told you they have been that way for centuries, hence the name, and you hypothesized that the cold is the result of one of the warlocks.

Hulrik had to dissuade you from climbing into the mountains to search for a centuries-old body buried deep under the snow somewhere along the mountain range, without any clear hints to its location or proof that it even exists. You probably would have died in the snow before you found anything. Instead, you decided to investigate Mirror Lake after hearing of a strange sighting there. Something large disturbed the surface, looking like a huge chunk of ice. But it didn't float and sunk back down into the depths. Kitt turned into a fish and swam down to take a look. She found a deep gouge in the bottom that looked like some creature pushing off the ground. Leaving Leroy and Ziolda far behind, Kitt kept swimming down until she found a steep drop off to nearly black water and finally turned back.

When you left the Mror Holds on the lightning rail, you dropped off Kitt and Izzy to go meet with Weyla and Silvari in Lakeside. There, Leroy meditated by the lakeside, trying to connect with his wife and daughter again and share his concern that Arel is a werewolf. He received a sense of encouragement to go find Arel.

Kitt and Izzy brought Weyla to Vedykar. There, she found Ulara, the current leader of House Jorasco and Kitt's aunt. Ulara told Kitt about her mother and Kitt informed Ulara of her sister's fate. Ulara gave Kitt Katija's signet ring, which she had left behind when she left Vedykar. Kitt, Weyla, and Izzy spent some time with chaos potions. Ulara gave you free healing potions and tried to heal Izzy's voice, but even her best healers could not.

Leroy brought Alkes to Cardel Keep after he expressed interest in joining the Crimson Knights. While Alkes looked into mutagens, Leroy informed Orlan, Bervodd, and Vitara of the other warlocks of Khyber that he intended to hunt. Orlan took Leroy aside and told him that the Blood of Vol seems to have developed something similar to the blood hunter mutagens that allows them to see in magical darkness. Crimson Knights were ambushed and even those with enhanced vision couldn't see through the darkness to fight back and were killed. He wanted Leroy to be aware of this because of your history with the Blood of Vol and because they suspect that a Crimson Knight who went missing years ago has joined the Blood of Vol. Leroy and Alkes got to work trying to figure out what kind of mutagen would have allowed this and, in the process, Alkes developed a mutagen that Leroy enhanced with his warlock magic. This mutagen healed the wounds Alkes had from the battle with Festarik. Leroy also discussed the Crimson Knights sending an expedition into the Mournlands, headed by the warforged.

Ziolda went to Kassandra's monastery and worked with her to find some sort of connection between her body and her soul that is possessing it from within her pendant. Eventually, Ziolda managed to get to a place similar to where the other paladins exist in their own sort of realms. She could see that Tira's and Inarieth's were projected on a flat surface like in Alderdia's diamond, and it struck her how cold and dark her realm is compared to the others with sun and flames. Realizing that she was in her pendant and remembering the vision about a pool of molten silver, Ziolda set up a bunch of mirrors to focus sunlight on her pendant until it melted, revealing that the core holding her soul had become a Siberys dragonshard. As the sunlight hit the pendant, it channeled through her to heal Kassandra.

Session 33

Last time, you finished up your month apart. Kitt was able to heal Izzy by learning Greater Restoration from her aunt. Alkes gave Leroy a puzzle sphere with a charged Khyber dragonshard in the center so he could bribe Oswald. And Ziolda worked with Kassandra to find information about ancient Solamnia.

Ziolda learned that Khardam Silvereye discovered his paladin powers when he was a squire brought along when Knights of the Rose went to fight the dragon Kymrys. Khardam empowered the knights around him and together they killed the dragon. But the dragon eventually came back, undead and leading an army of zombies. Khardam led the charge again and killed the dragon, but he was killed in the process. His body was never recovered, and the crypt Ziolda found under the college was always empty, more of a memorial to Khardam.

While there was no indication of where the battle took place, there were vague suggestions that it was south of Lake Palanthas, which was renamed Lake Galifar hundreds of years later.

You all traveled to Greenheart in the Eldeen Reaches and were directed to Oalian, The Great Druid. It turned out that Oalian is a massive tree. As you approached, Kitt sensed that ley lines spread out from beneath the tree as if it's an epicenter of ley line magic.

Through telepathy and Leroy's first use of Dream Glimpse, you figured out that although Oalian and Traelor were friends, Traelor became corrupted by Khyber and turned away from Siberys. Ziolda found the area of the forest scarred by cursed flames where Oalian and Traelor fought. Traelor ultimately won, turning Oalian into a tree.

You also found out that Oalian's spellbook contains the secret knowledge of how to use ley lines, but the location of the spellbook was lost when Oalian was defeated. Oalian also tried to impart that to use ley magic you also need a special lilac gem known as Eberron's Heart. Kitt picked up something like this while you were in Khyber's domain of the Great Diamond, and she was the only one it reacted to, though that was a vision of sorts, not the real thing.

Kitt learned some higher-level spells from the druids of Greenheart, and you traveled south to meet with Flynt in a village called Greenblade. Upon entering the local tavern, where Flynt and four Silver Moon captains were waiting, you noticed a sword in a display case above the bar and fixated on it.

Leroy paid the tavern owner to let him stand on the bar with Kitt on his shoulders. Meanwhile, Ziolda quickly got drunk and cast Detect Magic on the sword. She got a sense that whatever magic it once possessed was withered, much like the roses that were said to grow from the ornamental vines on the scabbard. Leroy saw that there was writing among the vines, but not in a language he recognized, and it didn't look like runes for a spell. The once-green blade also looked corroded.

As part of the payment, Leroy and Kitt got the tavern owner to agree to let Kitt cast a single spell on the sword so long as she didn't touch it. The plan was to cast Greater Restoration on the sword. Instead, she cast Druidcraft to make one of the roses grow. The rose grew but then quickly died. So Kitt cast Speak with Plants, then Druidcraft again. This time the rose grew, screamed in pain, and died.

At this point Kitt had cast three spells, none of which were the planned Greater Restoration. She realized that she needed to touch the sword to cast Greater Restoration, so you had to try to convince the tavern owner to let you open the glass case.

Ziolda decided that the sword just needed some water and threw a cup of water at the closed glass case. She missed and hit Leroy instead and then fell off of her stool.

When you were allowed to open the case, Kitt cast Greater Restoration only to realize that it only affects creatures. You were all promptly asked to leave, but you made plans to send Oswald in for the sword after you left town the next morning in exchange for the puzzle sphere. He was given explicit instructions not to blow anything up, not to burn anything down, and not to kill anyone, though a little bit of blood is okay.
