Power in this age is not held by crowns alone. It is held by the Houses — sprawling
family dynasties marked, quite literally, by destiny.

## The Marks

Members of these bloodlines sometimes manifest a **dragonmark**: an intricate,
living sigil upon the skin that grants subtle magical talents. A house built
around a mark of healing runs the hospitals; one bearing the mark of passage
runs the caravans and the lightning rail.

## Neutral Ground

By ancient compact the Houses took no side in the Last War, selling their services
to all comers. That neutrality made them rich, and made them dangerous.

*Adapt the houses, their marks, and their politics to fit your campaign.*
