The Chronicle

Session Recaps

The story so far, told arc by arc. Open an arc to read its sessions and interludes in order.

Arc 1 · Sessions 1–4

Blood on the Lake

Three strangers, each scarred by the Mourning, are drawn together in Karrnath — hunting the dead that rise around Lakeside and the secrets drowned beneath Lake Dark.

Arc 2 · Sessions 5–10

Unrest in Thaliost

Chasing the Silver Flame's buried history with werewolves, the party uncovers the hidden Silver Moon and is pulled into the unrest of occupied Thaliost.

Arc 3 · Sessions 11–16

The Dark Pack

Riding with the Silver Moon into the Graywall Mountains, the heroes take the fight to the werewolves of the Dark Pack — and learn the Emerald Claw hunts the Fount of Inarieth.

Arc 4 · Sessions 17–21

The Fount of Inarieth

A desperate race across the Blade Desert to Q'barra, to reach the fabled Fount of Inarieth before the Order of the Emerald Claw.

Arc 5 · Sessions 22–25

The Pirates of Lhazaar

Among the pirates of the Lhazaar Principalities, the party chases a star map to the lost island of Draksholm — and wakes something ancient in a hidden lab.

Arc 6 · Sessions 26–33

The Ruins of Draksholm

On the dead island of Draksholm, the heroes walk the ruins with the last of its lords, gathering ancient relics and the first secrets of the Draconic Prophecy.

Arc 7 · Sessions 34–45

Legacy of Solamnia

From the haunted Gloaming and a buried Solamnic fortress to the edge of the Mournlands, a fallen kingdom's legacy shapes the war to come — as Ziolda's legend grows.

Arc 8 · Sessions 46–47

Forged by War

Deep in the Mournlands, the party infiltrates the warforged dominion rising at Eston, where the first war-machines were forged — and sparks a desperate uprising.
