Religion

The Progenitor Dragons

Siberys

Siberys is the creator of light and souls. He is the source of divine magic in the world.

Siberys dragonshards are used as material components for powerful spells, most notably the Revivify spell. They also power the network of sending stones across Khorvaire. They are the rarest of the three major dragonshards and can only be obtained when they fall to earth from the sky.

Siberys was shattered by Khyber when she betrayed him and Eberron. He only survived because of his close ties to the diamond dragon Alderdia, who now acts as his conduit in the world.

Siberys is known by many names, including Dol Arrah in ancient Solamnia, Arawai among the sea elves of Lorghalan, the Silver Flame in Galifar, and Aureon among the ancient elves.

Associated colors: Gold (and silver via Alderdia)

Eberron

Eberron is the creator of life and matter. She is the source of primal magic in the world.

Eberron dragonshards are used to sustain low-level magical effects over very long periods. They are quite common in the crust of the world, particularly in Q'barra and the Shadow Marches.

When Khyber betrayed Eberron and Siberys, Eberron bound her in a magical prison said to be at the center of the world. Eberron has gradually weakened under the strain of maintaining that prison, until she has fallen into an essentially comatose state.

Associated color: Purple

Khyber

Khyber is the creator of energy and the forces that hold the creations of Siberys and Eberron together. She is the source of arcane magic in the world.

Khyber dragonshards are used to store elemental energy, usually to power constructs like airships, warforged, and the lightning rail. They are reasonably common, though they must be mined from deeper in the earth.

Since Khyber was bound in Eberron's prison, she has spitefully sent out tendrils of her magic, making pacts and manipulating the people of Khorvaire to oppose Siberys and Eberron at any chance she gets.

Associated color: Blue

Others

Zaverys

Zaverys is the son of Siberys and Eberron, with the potential to replace Khyber if she is finally defeated.

Zaverys dragonshards, called obsidian dragonshards by those who don't know of his existence, are found only in the land of Cyre. They became more common there after the Mourning, which transmuted the plateau beneath the city of Making into a range of jagged obsidian. The Lord of Blades has an extensive mining operation to collect them. They are the most powerful of all dragonshards, with properties similar to each of the other three and greater potency.

The cause of the Mourning appears to be experimentation by Cyran artificers to combine the more common Khyber dragonshards with the more potent obsidian dragonshards. Unaware of the true nature of the dragonshards and the associated gods, the results were extremely unstable and unleashed a blast of magic across the land.

Associated colors: Black (major), red and orange (minor)

Alderdia

Alderdia was the first dragon created and the oldest living being in the world. He resides in the Great Diamond on the island of Draksholm.

Millennia ago, the Progenitor Dragons assigned some dragons, including Alderdia, to watch over the dragonborn of Draksholm. Alderdia would assume the form of a diamond dragonborn and walk among them, helping to guide them.

When Khyber betrayed the others and tried to kill Siberys, it was Alderdia's close bond with Siberys that kept the god alive, despite being shattered across the heavens. Alderdia now serves as the conduit of Siberys's power into the world, and as the source of the bond between Ziolda and the other paladins. Ziolda's state of imperfect resurrection allows Alderdia to connect her soul to the souls of the fallen paladins, though the paladins cannot connect to each other directly through these bonds.

When Festarik and his draconians nearly destroyed Draksholm, Alderdia was resting within the Great Diamond. He put forth a great deal of his power to combat Khyber's magic and allow the remaining dragonborn to escape, before pulling the island into a demiplane and stranding the draconians until their deaths. This act, however, eternally bound Alderdia to the interior of the Great Diamond.

Alderdia spent the millennia trying to orchestrate a scenario to finally kill Khyber, by gathering three champions — each wielding the power of one of the Progenitor Dragons — strong enough to kill her. With Eberron weakened by the long effort to contain Khyber, all Khyber needs to escape is to withdraw her strength from her warlocks and focus it on breaking her bonds. Alderdia wants the champions to grow in strength, taking lessons and power from the champions before them and defeating Khyber's warlocks, so that when Khyber realizes she is strong enough to break free, she will do so only to find the champions ready to kill her. These plans have been unknowingly thwarted several times by Khyber, but now the party has the best chance Alderdia could hope for.

The remaining flaw in his plan, however, is that arcane magic and all of its creations — including the very forces that hold the world together — will start to crumble without Khyber. The party has found a potential solution in the discovery of Zaverys, the son of Eberron and Siberys. Based on the properties of Zaverys dragonshards, Zaverys has the potential to wield arcane magic and to take Khyber's place.
