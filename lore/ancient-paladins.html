Ancient Paladins — Champions of Siberys

The great paladins of ages past, whose spirits have reached across the centuries to arm Ziolda.

  • Category Legends

History Champions of Siberys

Tira Miron

  • Race Human woman
  • Oath Paladin of the Silver Flame
  • Location Thrane

Significant Enemies

  • The Dark Pack: Tira defeated the leader of the werewolves, Valdren, but was unable to eradicate the rest. The remnants of the Dark Pack went into hiding to regain their strength and bring Valdren's spirit back as the Umbral Shade.
  • House Vol: Tira led a crusade for the Church of the Silver Flame against the necromancers of House Vol, who were deemed heretical by the Church. She was called away from the fight during the final siege by the imminent threat of the Great Demon attacking Thrane.
  • The Great Demon: When an army of demonic creatures from the Demon Wastes managed to escape the Labyrinth and invade Thrane, Tira sacrificed herself to defeat the Great Demon. They were both immolated in a pillar of silver fire. Rumors tell of the Great Demon's return in the heart of the Demon Wastes.

Legacy

The pillar of silver fire remains several centuries later. The city of Flamekeep was built around the flames, and a statue of Tira was placed next to them in the heart of the chief cathedral of the Church of the Silver Flame.

Tira's face was also carved into a cliff along the road in northern Thrane. Pilgrims regularly make journeys to the Face of Tira, and some claim to have received visions from her there.

Spiritual Gift to ZioldaTira gave Ziolda her shield, which manifests as Ziolda's Aura of Protection.

Inarieth

  • Race Sea elf woman
  • Oath Paladin of Arawai
  • Location Lorghalan

Significant Enemies

  • The Elder Tempest: Inarieth died defeating a massive elemental that was attacking the sea elves on the island of Lorghalan. Many years later, the sea elf capital — named Inarion in her honor — was destroyed by a Leviathan, and the sea elves were nearly driven to extinction.

Legacy

Inarieth found a magical spring on the island of Lorghalan that was later named the Fount of Inarieth. It has powerful healing properties and is closely connected to both Siberys and Eberron.

Inarieth's sword, charged with lightning energy from her battle, was lost to the seas of the Tempest Strait. It was later found by Ziolda and given to Inarianna, Inarieth's descendant and the last of the sea elves.

Spiritual Gift to ZioldaInarieth gave Ziolda her gauntlets, which manifest as Ziolda's Divine Allegiance.

Khardam Silvereye

  • Race Human man
  • Oath Paladin of Dol Arrah
  • Location Solamnia

Significant Enemies

  • Kymrys: Khardam killed the evil dragon Kymrys when he first manifested his divine powers. Years later, a resurrected Kymrys led an army of the undead against Solamnia. Khardam led the Solamnians in the battle and died defeating Kymrys. His body was stolen by the enemy, and a monument was built at the site of his death. Without his leadership, the kingdom of Solamnia eventually crumbled from within.

Legacy

Khardam's bones were obtained by the Cult of the Dragon Below. Beneath the Blackcap Mountains, they sacrificed dozens of draconians to perform a blood magic ritual, rebuilding Khardam's body but using it as a vessel for Kymrys's spirit.

In a Solamnian crypt unearthed near Arcanix, Ziolda found a tomb with Khardam's name on it, though it was empty. The tomb was among other knights who belonged to the Order of the Rose. Though it no longer exists, Ziolda joined this order in spirit.

Spiritual Gift to ZioldaKhardam gave Ziolda his helm, which manifests as her Aura of Courage.

Traelor

  • Race Half-elf man
  • Oath Paladin of Aureon
  • Location Eldeen Reaches

Significant Enemies

  • The monsters of the Demon Wastes: Traelor used arcane magic from his research at Arcanix to raise the Labyrinth, an impassable maze of stone, to contain the horrors of the Demon Wastes. Centuries later, the Great Demon managed to get a force of monsters past the Labyrinth to invade Thrane.
  • Oalian, the Great Druid: Traelor made a pact with Khyber in his effort to raise the Labyrinth, and this resulted in him turning on his friend Oalian. Their battle in the Eldeen Reaches ended with Oalian being turned into a tree, and Traelor disappeared.

Legacy

The alliance between Oalian and Traelor was the only time in history that the champions of Siberys and Eberron teamed up against Khyber — until Kitt and Ziolda. It was the closest the world had come to all three champions aligning, until now.
