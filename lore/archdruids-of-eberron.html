Oalian, “The Great Druid”

  • Form Formerly a human man — now a tree

Oalian was a friend of the paladin Traelor and an ally in the fight against the monsters of the Demon Wastes. He was present when Traelor created the Labyrinth to seal off the Demon Wastes from the rest of Khorvaire.

After Traelor turned from Siberys to Khyber, Oalian confronted him, and the resulting battle destroyed a swath of the Towering Wood. Traelor defeated Oalian and transmuted him into a tree instead of killing him.

The tree still stands centuries later, towering over the surrounding woods and giving the plants there the power to thrive. The druidic village of Greenheart was established nearby as a result.

Oalian was a master of ley magic, tapping into the latent power of Eberron in the earth itself. The art of ley magic was lost after his transformation, and his spellbook disappeared along with Traelor after the battle. Kitt discovered that ley lines spread out in all directions from Oalian's tree and into the forest — likely the means of spreading his power through the region.

Eladan Ardosar

  • Race Elf man

Eladan was both a druid and a wizard who discovered the magical anomaly known as the Snarl and built the magical school of Arcanix above it.

He discovered, and put a stop to, the secret experiments going on at Arcanix that were creating the mutated beings later known as the Deeplings. The “failed experiments” were dumped into the waters of Lake Galifar, where they secretly created their own underwater society with Eladan's aid.

Eladan was also a talented necromancer who founded the Order of the Black Robes at Arcanix, which Kitt later joined.
