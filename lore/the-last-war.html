The Last War

  • Category History

History War

For more than a hundred years the continent bled. What began as a war of succession became a war of survival, drawing in every nation and ending only when an entire land was unmade in a single day.

"We did not win the war. We merely ran out of reasons to keep fighting." — attributed to a weary general at the signing of the peace.

The Cause

When the old king died without a clear heir, five rival claimants each declared themselves the rightful successor. Treaties failed, alliances curdled, and the realm fractured along the lines that still divide it today.

The Peace

The fighting ended with a fragile treaty rather than a true victory. The borders it drew are watched by suspicious eyes, and few believe the peace will last.
