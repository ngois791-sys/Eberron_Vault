Veyl the Broker

  • Role Fixer & Information Broker
  • Race Half-elf
  • Location The City of Towers
  • Status Alive — whereabouts unknown

NPC City of Towers

A thin, well-dressed half-elf who speaks quickly and glances at the door too often. Veyl trades in secrets and lost things, and is never quite as poor as he pretends.

What the Party Knows

  • He hired them to recover a stolen lockbox without explaining what's inside.
  • He paid half up front, in unmarked coin.
