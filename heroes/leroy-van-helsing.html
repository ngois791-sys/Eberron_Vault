Hero of the Realm

Leroy Van Helsing

A blood-sworn knight hunting the werewolves that destroyed his family.

  • Player (player name)
  • Class Blood Hunter
  • Order Crimson Knights of Khorvaire
  • Status Active — changed by the Mourning

Hero The Dark Pack

Years ago, werewolves killed Leroy's wife and daughter and carried his son away into the dark. He found the Crimson Knights of Khorvaire and gave himself over to their cause: to hunt the things that hunt the innocent.

The Hunt

Leroy and Ronan infiltrated wartime Cyre on the trail of the Dark Pack — the werewolves who had taken everything from him. There he was caught in the mists of the Mourning, which changed him in ways he is still learning. Having now completed the hemocraft rite to become a full Crimson Knight, he rides east to investigate Lake Dark.

Family

  • His wife and daughter, killed by the Dark Pack.
  • His daughter, Aurora.
  • His son, Arel — carried off by the werewolves, and perhaps still out there.

What Drives Him

  • Find his son.
  • Hunt down the Dark Pack, root and branch.
  • Master the blood magic without losing himself — and learn what the Mourning made of him.
