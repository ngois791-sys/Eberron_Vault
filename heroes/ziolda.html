Hero of the Realm

Ziolda

A knight of the Sacred Flame who walked back from death — and the chosen Champion of Siberys.

  • Player (player name)
  • Class Paladin
  • Devotion Church of the Sacred Flame
  • Status Active — returned from death

Hero Champions of Siberys

Ziolda died — and did not stay dead. Killed and reanimated, she spent a long, blurred span moving through the world without truly controlling it, her body carrying her toward Flamekeep while her mind slept. Now awake and whole again, she sets out on a mission for the Church of the Sacred Flame.

The Champion of Siberys

The spirits of three ancient paladins have reached across the centuries to arm her:

  • Tira Miron's shield — her Aura of Protection.
  • Inarieth's gauntlets — her Divine Allegiance.
  • Khardam Silvereye's helm — her Aura of Courage.

She recovered Inarieth's lost sword from the Tempest Strait and returned it to Inarianna, the last of the sea elves. Near Arcanix she found Khardam's empty tomb among the knights of the vanished Order of the Rose, and joined that order in spirit.

What Drives Her

  • Carry out the mission the Church has given her.
  • Understand what she has become, and what brought her back.
  • Live up to the champions whose relics she now bears.
