Hero Champions of Eberron

Kitt left her tribe to make the long journey to Arcanix, the great school of magic. On the road she found Peanut — and the bones of a dragon, which she shaped into a staff. In that moment her latent dragonmark awakened.

Champion of Eberron

Where Ziolda is named a Champion of Siberys, Kitt walks the path of Eberron. Not since Traelor and Oalian, ages ago, have the champions of both powers stood together against the dark — until now (see the Ancient Paladins).

What Drives Her

  • Reach Arcanix and learn to wield her gift.
  • Understand her dragonmark and where it came from.
  • Discover what, exactly, Peanut is.
