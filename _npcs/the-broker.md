A thin, well-dressed half-elf who speaks quickly and glances at the door too often.
Veyl trades in secrets and lost things, and is never quite as poor as he pretends.

## What the Party Knows

- He hired them to recover a stolen lockbox without explaining what's inside.
- He paid half up front, in unmarked coin.
